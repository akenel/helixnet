## LOGLINE
Mike asks for *"a little help"* moving his garage. It turns out to be a 1,000-lb car crane that
needs **twenty hands** to lift — so a help-post becomes a community **event**, held together by
his **housebound sister Sally's cookies**.

## THE SHAPE (Sid Field)
- **Plot (A-story) — THE MOVE.** Get the 1,000-lb crane out. Needs ~10 heavy lifters / 20 hands,
  ~50 kg a head — *fit people only.* Stakes: it can't be done alone; Saturday is the day.
- **Subplot (B-story) — THE COOKIES.** Sally (Mike's sister, wheelchair, can't leave the house)
  bakes. Maria couriers them. The cookies are the **lure** (they draw the crew) and the **glue**
  (they give the housebound sister a way to belong). *"Bake extras, girl."*
- **Theme.** A neighbourhood that builds together. Everyone lifts *something* — even the one who
  can't lift at all.

## THE BEATS — and the feature each one tests
### ACT 1 — SETUP
1. **Mike posts on the Help Board:** *"Need Help Moving a Garage (Disassembly/Relocation)."*
   → tests: **Help Board post** (type=NEED).
2. The crew replies — Nino, Sally (remote + cookies), Marco (+friend), Jake, Maria.
   → tests: **replies / threads / upvotes**, multi-user.

### ACT 2 — CONFRONTATION (it's bigger than "a little help")
3. The reveal: it's a **1,000-lb crane**, 20 hands, fit lifters only. A help-post can't hold this.
4. **The pivot (organic + true):** *"you can't see the picture on the help board → so I'll make an
   EVENT"* — proper photos, a date, RSVP. → tests: **Event create** (date, venue, capacity 0/12).
5. **Cookie logistics (the subplot, in DMs):** Mike ↔ Sally (bake), Mike ↔ Maria (pick up & deliver).
   → tests: **private messaging**, the side sub-story running parallel to the public thread.
6. **Recruiting / the growth loop:** Marco brings a friend; Mike recruits Elena (trade the bowls).
   "Get your guys to join so they can see the event." → tests: **share the link → new signup → RSVP**.

### ACT 3 — RESOLUTION (Saturday)
7. **The lift.** 20 hands on the crane. The cookies arrive (Maria delivers Sally's batch).
8. **The payoff.** The crane's out, new neighbours met, Sally belonged from her kitchen.
   The *move* is done; the *community* is forged.

## 🔎 PRODUCT INSIGHTS this story surfaced (real notes, not fiction)
- **Events need RSVP, members-only.** To RSVP you must be a La Piazza member — that's the key of
  events. An **open event** is fine: 2 people > nobody.
- **No "+1 / add a guest" feature.** A host can't add guests. **Workaround = share the link, invite
  friends, they sign up + RSVP.** → *Future feature candidate: "invite a friend / +guest" on events.*
- **"Can't see the picture on the Help Board" → make an Event** (proper photos). The help-board
  media gap *drove* the pivot — worth checking if help-posts should show images better.

  screenshotted at 1920×1080; stitch intro + body + outro.
- **MP4s → GDrive**, not git. Description: no `< >`, no `->`, chapters from `0:00`.
