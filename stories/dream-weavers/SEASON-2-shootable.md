## Logline
It's Tuesday, the move is Saturday, and only **6 of 12** have signed up. So the crew prints the
event as **Locandina postcards** with a QR, gets them into hands, and every scan turns a stranger
into a member who RSVPs and brings a friend — until the event fills and the crane gets lifted.

## The real-feature spine (kept Sid's grammar: flyer → sign your name → it lights up the app)
We keep the master's **visual loop** — printed cue → personal sign-up → the app updates — and swap his
silent-era "town ledger" for our **QR → become a member → RSVP.** We DROP his invented "+guest": the
real (and better) beat is **share the link → the friend signs up themselves → RSVPs.** That's the onboarding.
