## LOGLINE
A newcomer with no friends in town clicks one shared link — and by helping a stranger named Mike
lift a 1,000-lb crane out of his garage, discovers a neighbourhood that builds together… and learns
to build their own.

## THEME (state it in the first shot, pay it off in the last)
> **Everyone lifts something — even the one who can't lift at all.**

## THE THREE STRANDS
- **A-STORY · the PLOT (the spine):** *the Move.* A 1,000-lb car crane. 20 hands. One Saturday.
  Question that pulls every episode: **will enough people show up to lift it?**
- **B-STORY · the HEART (the subplot):** *Sally.* Mike's sister — wheelchair, housebound, can't
  come. But she **bakes**, and Maria couriers the cookies. The one who can't lift holds it all
  together. Pays off at the climax.
- **C-STORY · the THREAT (keeps it honest):** *the Scammer.* Someone tries to funnel the crew
  off-platform ("€500, DM me on Telegram"). The community + the safety-scan catch it. The threat
  proves the warmth is *protected*, not naïve.

## THE PROTAGONIST — and the arc (this is the emotional engine)
**The Newcomer** = the viewer's proxy. New in town, knows no one. Arc across the season:
> **stranger → curious → helper → belonging → BUILDER.**
By Ep 12 the person who arrived alone is opening *their own* workshop. *That's* the conversion —
emotional, then literal (sign up → build your own).

## THE ENSEMBLE
Mike (host, big heart, in over his head) · **Sally** (the heart) · Maria (the powerhouse + cookie
courier) · Marco (loyal — *brings a friend* = the growth) · Nino & Jake (the crew) · Elena (the
trade: bowls for muscle) · **the Newcomer** (us / you) · **the Scammer** (the antagonist beat).

## 🎥 THE FIRST SHOT (Ep 1, second 1)
**An empty town square at first light** — nobody there yet, just stone and morning. A phone on a
café table **buzzes**: a shared card (a Banksy `/s/` link to Mike's event) glows on the screen. A
hand picks it up. *"…what's this?"* — **the hook.** (Theme stated: a community, not yet gathered,
one link away.)

## 📖 THE PREFACE — "Episode 0 / The Map" (60–90s cold-open trailer)
Before Ep 1, a **Table of Contents** the viewer sees once: *"This is La Piazza — a town square with
no fees, no middlemen. Over 12 short episodes, watch a neighbourhood move a 1,000-pound crane with
twenty hands and a tray of one housebound woman's cookies — and learn, step by step, to build your
own town square. Here's the map."* → fast cuts of the season's icons (help board · event · the lift
· the cookies). Ends: *"Pull up a chair. The square's about to fill."* **The hook + the map, in 90s.**

## 🎬 THE 3-ACT SEASON MAP (Sid Field paradigm over 12 eps)
**ACT I — SETUP (Eps 1–3): the square, the call, the crew gathers.**
- E1 Welcome · E2 Ask the Neighbourhood (Mike's help-post) · E3 The Crew Rallies.
- **PLOT POINT 1 (end of E3):** the reveal — *"it's not a garage… it's a 1,000-lb crane, and it
  needs twenty hands."* No turning back. A help-post can't hold this.

**ACT II — CONFRONTATION (Eps 4–9): make it real, and bigger than one person.**
- E4 Make an Event · E5 RSVP & open it up · E6 The Side Conversation (Sally's cookies, in DMs).
- **MIDPOINT (E6–7):** the crew alone **isn't enough** → they must *grow.* E7 Grow the Crowd
  (Marco's friend, invite-a-friend, the share card). E8 The Trade (Elena: bowls for muscle).
- **PLOT POINT 2 (E9):** the **Scammer** strikes — a fake "I'll move it for €500, DM me" funnel
  threatens to splinter the crew. The lowest point / the doubt. The scan + the neighbours catch it.

**ACT III — RESOLUTION (Eps 10–12): the lift, and the new dawn.**
- E10 Who's Coming? (the Who-Viewed panel — turn lookers into the last hands needed).
- **E11 — THE CLIMAX: THE LIFT.** Saturday. Twenty hands on the crane. Maria arrives with Sally's
  cookies. *On three…* — and it **rises.** The crane up = the community up. The Newcomer, a stranger
  eleven episodes ago, is shoulder-to-shoulder with the crew. Sally, who never left her kitchen,
  **is the one who held it together.** Theme paid off.
- **E12 — The New Dawn / build your own.**

## 🎥 THE END SHOT (Ep 12, last frame) — mirrors the first
**Golden hour. The same square — now FULL.** The crew, the bare patch where the crane stood, the
Newcomer handing out the **last of Sally's cookies.** Then: the Newcomer opens a laptop and posts
**their own first listing.** Pull back — **other squares light up across a map.** *One town becomes
many.* Title card: **"Build your own. La Piazza."** (Dawn-empty → dusk-full → multiplied.)

## 🪝 THE RULE OF THE HOOK (the magic)
**End every episode on the cookies or the crane** — never on a feature summary. The feature is
*taught in service of the beat.* Short. Slick. Each episode = its own OG share card (a still + a
one-line tease) so every drop is shareable. *They stay for the story; they learn the tool by
accident.*
