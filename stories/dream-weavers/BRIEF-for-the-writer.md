# Dream Weavers — Brief for the Master Writer
*Context to hand a Legends master so they can write the next chapter. Season 1 is done; we go forward.*

## What this is
**Dream Weavers** is a series of short, silent, Sid-Field-structured episodes that demo a real
software platform (**La Piazza** — an open-source neighbourhood marketplace, no fees, no middlemen,
no tracking) by telling a story *through the app itself*. Each episode teaches one feature by showing
neighbours actually using it. The story is also the QA test and the onboarding. No voiceover — the
on-screen action + title/outro cards carry it. ~20–60s per episode.

## Season 1 — "Mike's Big Move" (12 episodes, DONE, do NOT rewrite)
**Logline:** A newcomer clicks one shared link and, by helping a stranger named Mike lift a 1,000-lb
crane out of his garage with twenty hands and a tray of his housebound sister Sally's cookies,
discovers a neighbourhood that builds together — and learns to build their own.
- **Plot:** the move (the crane needs 20 hands, one Saturday). **Subplot:** Sally's cookies (she
  can't leave the house but feeds the crew). **Threat:** a scammer caught by the safety scan.
- **Cast:** Mike (host), Sally (sister, cookies), Maria (moves buildings + cookie courier), Marco
  (+a friend), Nino, Jake, Elena (trades singing bowls for muscle), the Newcomer.
- **The 12:** Welcome → Ask the Neighbourhood → The Crew Rallies → Make an Event → RSVP & Open It Up
  → The Side Conversation (DMs) → Grow the Crowd (share/QR) → The Trade → Spot the Scammer → Who's
  Coming (who-viewed) → The Lift (climax) → Build Your Own Piazza (CTA).

## The format (keep it)
Silent. Sid Field 3-act feel even at episode scale (setup → turn → payoff). Intro card (episode title
+ a one-line hook), the screen-recorded beat, outro card (next episode + subscribe). End each episode
on a hook (the cookies or the crane). Each episode is self-contained AND part of the never-ending story.

## The features still to showcase (raw material for the writer)
Profile/Bottega building (CV → bio), the **Locandina** (print a digital listing as a physical
postcard/poster with a QR — the bridge to the real world), Raffles, the Daily One-Pager, the Legends
(ask-a-master mentors — this very feature), reviews, disputes, trades, multiple addresses.

## What we want from you (the master writer)
A **Season 2** in the same format — the *real* live scramble: it's Tuesday, the move is Saturday,
only 6 of 12 have signed up. Print the Locandina postcards, get them into hands, people scan the QR →
must become members to RSVP (you can only RSVP yourself — that's the design, taught as onboarding) →
everyone brings a friend → the event fills. Tie digital → physical (the printed card) → digital.
Give us: a Season-2 logline, the new beat (what feature each episode teaches), and Episode 13's
shooting beats (Sid Field structure, ~30–45s, silent, intro/outro card lines).
