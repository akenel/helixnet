The lane nobody else is in

Small CH/IT cross-border, multi-fiscal, SOP-shaped, owner-at-the-counter.

The incumbents can't customize for one head shop in Littau — they serve millions they'll never meet. The big ERPs (SAP B1, Odoo, Zoho) are too heavy and too far away. That leaves a real, empty gap: a small cross-border till that speaks German and Italian, handles two countries' fiscal rules, and is built by someone who sits next to the shopkeeper. In that lane, Banco isn't competing. It's alone.
