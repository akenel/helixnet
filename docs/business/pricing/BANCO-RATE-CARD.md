# Banco / HelixNet — Rate Card (internal)

*Swiss solo-operator rates. Tradesman, not Big-4. The wedge: SAP-class operations at trade prices,
no per-user fees. 2026-06-25 · pairs with [[solo-founder-plan]] + [[steward-guild-why-now]].*

> **The anchor:** a Swiss plumber/electrician bills CHF 90–130/hr; a Big-4 / SAP consultant CHF 250–400/hr.
> We do genius-tier software at **skilled-tradesman rates.** That's the pitch.

## Hourly — the steward rate
| Tier | Rate |
|---|---|
| Small shop / friend | CHF 90–100/hr |
| **Standard** | **CHF 120/hr** |
| Complex maker-retailer (winery/dairy) | CHF 140–150/hr |

Never the $50 trap. Big changes are hourly; small tweaks ride the retainer.

## Go-Live package — flat, scoped (one-time)
Protects both sides from open-ended hours. Pick by complexity:
| Profile | Price |
|---|---|
| Simple — start empty / sell-to-seed, 1–2 tills | CHF 1,200–1,800 |
| Standard — catalogue load, multi-cashier, payment methods, parallel run | CHF 2,000–3,000 |
| Complex — tiered/tour pricing, production, wholesale (the winery) | CHF 3,500–6,000 |

Includes: real-identity setup, VAT config, cashier accounts, payment methods, catalogue plan, sandbox
rehearsal, ~3-day parallel run, go-live, week-1 hypercare. (See the onboarding checklist.)

## Steward Care — monthly retainer (the MRR)
| Size | Rate |
|---|---|
| Small | CHF 60–80/mo |
| Complex | CHF 120–150/mo |

Covers: monthly check-in, updates, small tweaks, on-call. Big changes billed hourly.

## Hosting
- **Self-host:** their server, their cost (~CHF 10–30/mo). Cleanest liability for us.
- **Managed (recommended):** CHF 30–60/mo — we control the environment, recurring.

## The wedge — no per-user
SAP Business One charges **per named user** (roughly CHF 100–180/user/mo, or a perpetual licence into the
thousands) **+ an implementation partner at CHF 200–300/hr + ~20% annual maintenance.** A 3-cashier shop:
easily **CHF 8,000–15,000 year one**, thousands ongoing, per-user forever.

**Ours:** package once + retainer + hosting + occasional hourly → **~CHF 2,000 year one, ~CHF 1,400/yr after,
add staff for free.** *(SAP figures are rough — verify before quoting in a deck.)*

**One-liner:** *"SAP would cost you ten grand the first year and charge per cashier forever. Mine's a
couple thousand, and staff are free to add."*

## How to quote
1. Pick the **package** by complexity (above).
2. Set the **retainer** by support level + size.
3. Choose **hosting** (default to managed for non-technical clients).
4. Hourly for anything outside the package.
5. **Charge every customer** — first one too. (Felix = founder/reference rate, see ARTEMIS-FELIX-PROPOSAL.)
