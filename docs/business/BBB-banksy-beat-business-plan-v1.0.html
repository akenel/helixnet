The Funnel Math

10,000 free postcards distributed → 1,000 QR scans (10%) → 100 website visits (10%) → 10 purchases (10%) → €500 revenue

Cost per acquisition: If 10,000 postcards cost €500 to print = €50 per customer. But postcards keep working forever. Second scan = free. Third scan = free. CAC drops to near zero over time.
