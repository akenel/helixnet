# AK Stone & Landscape -- Sicily

*"Mountains of marble. Hands of a mason. The stone does the talking."*

## Business Concept
Premium natural stone masonry and landscaping services in western Sicily.
Marble and granite sourced direct from Custonaci quarries (20km from Trapani).

- **Base:** Trapani / Baglio Xiare area
