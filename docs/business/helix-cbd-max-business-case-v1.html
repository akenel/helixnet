The Partnership

This proposal formalizes an expansion of our existing partnership into the Italian CBD market, specifically targeting Sicily as a proof-of-concept territory.

The Opportunity

Sicily has no dedicated CBD presence in Trapani — a city of 70,000 with significant port traffic (Egadi Islands ferries) and zero competition.

The Solution

Helix CBD Max is a refrigerated vending operation with a three-tier product strategy:

Tier Products Role Margin
1. Foundation Cold beer, drinks, snacks Revenue stability Low-Medium
2. Core CBD Vapes (Felix production) Primary profit driver High
3. Premium CBD flower, oils, accessories Differentiation Medium-High
The Insight: Beer pays the rent. Vapes pay the profit. Flower builds loyalty. No single point of failure.

The 0.5% THC Strategy

Italian legal limit: 0.6% THC
Our target: 0.5% THC

Why the buffer? Lab variance, different equipment, police testing margin of error. At 0.5%, even with +0.05% variance, we're still compliant at 0.55%.

Why VAT Compliance Matters

The €20 Million Mistake (Real Story)
A major consulting firm built a system that calculated VAT BEFORE applying discounts. Result: customers overcharged, company liable for €20M+ in corrections.

❌ WRONG Method

Price: €100 VAT (22%): €22 Subtotal: €122 Discount: -€12.20 Total: €109.80 ← VAT on wrong base!

✓ RIGHT Method (HelixNet)

Price: €100 Discount: -€10 Subtotal: €90 VAT (22%): €19.80 Total: €109.80 ← VAT is CORRECT

The Hemp Ban Threat

Political Risk
Italian parliament proposed ban on hemp flower sales (late 2024). Not yet passed. If it passes, we have a 48-hour pivot plan.

48-Hour Pivot Plan

  1. Remove flower from machine
  2. Increase vape/oil inventory
  3. Add more beer/drinks slots
  4. Communicate with customers
  5. Continue operations without interruption

The Police Test Scenario

If Guardia di Finanza tests our product:

  • Every batch has Zurich lab certificate
  • Batch code → traceable to certificate
  • Our target (0.5%) is well under limit (0.6%)
  • ISO 9001 documentation shows compliance intent
