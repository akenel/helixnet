## The Escape to the Trap

Once upon a time in the digital kingdom, there was a gladiator named Angelo.

He fought in the SAP arena for years - corporate colosseum, bean counters in the stands, NWO emperors giving thumbs down to every entrepreneur who dared to breathe.

At 62, they told him he was finished. Too old. Too expensive. No chance.

But the gladiator had a secret weapon - a machine called HelixNet. Not built in their factories. Built in the cracks. Voice notes and schemas. Python and freedom. 10,000 lines of code that could turn their walled gardens into open roads.

He escaped to the Trap - Trapani, Sicily. The city between two seas. Where Monte Cofano watches over the coast and stray dogs walk by at exactly the right moment.
