UFA Client Intake

Fill in during client meeting -- Compilare durante l'incontro

1. Business Info / Dati Azienda

Get from Google Maps: long-press on location to copy coordinates

2. Product / Prodotto

3. Creative / Creativita

Ask the client: "Cosa rende speciale la vostra attivita?" (What makes your business special?)
Write down their answer -- it often becomes the quote or tagline.

4. Before Leaving / Prima di Andare

Photo of the business front / storefront
Photo of their business card (if they have one)
Google Maps location pinned + coordinates noted
Confirmed spelling of business name
Confirmed phone / email / website
Agreed on product type(s)
Client approved theme / quote direction
Discussed timeline for proof review
Contact method agreed (WhatsApp / email / in-person)
Exchanged WhatsApp numbers

5. Notes / Appunti

Quick Reference / Riferimento Rapido

Workflow after intake:
1. Copy the right template (tent-card or duplex-3card)
2. Fill in all [PLACEHOLDERS] from this form
3. Generate QR code from Google Maps link
4. Generate PDF: node scripts/postcard-to-pdf.js input.html output.pdf
5. Send proof to client (WhatsApp photo of PDF)
6. Get approval, print at ISOTTO, deliver
