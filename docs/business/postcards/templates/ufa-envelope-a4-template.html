UFA Envelope Folding Instructions

  1. FOLD 1 (Bottom): Fold the bottom section UP along the dashed line
  2. FOLD 2 (Left): Fold the left flap IN toward center
  3. FOLD 3 (Right): Fold the right flap IN toward center
  4. FOLD 4 (Top): Fold the top flap DOWN and TUCK inside

Tips:

  • Print on regular A4 paper (B&W is fine)
  • Fold lines are guides - fold slightly INSIDE the line
  • No glue needed - the tuck flap holds it closed
  • Write address on FRONT after folding
  • For extra security, use a small sticker or tape
