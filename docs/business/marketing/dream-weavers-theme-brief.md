# 🎸 The Dream Weavers Theme — Music Brief (Season 1)
*For the music-generation site. You make the song; Tigs masters + loops + scores the season.*

## THE VIBE
**Punchy folk-rock / Americana road-rock.** Warm, scrappy, hopeful, built-by-one-person.
A neighbourhood pulling together, the open road, the underdog who wins. Sicilian highway energy.
**Reference feel:** Tom Petty riff + The Lumineers stomp + a lonesome harmonica hook.

## THE INSTRUMENTS (the layers to add)
- **Electric/acoustic guitar** — the punchy lead riff (you've got this — it's the hook). Keep it front.
- **Harmonica** — the soul/melody line, the part people hum. Sits on the peaks.
- **Tambourine** — the drive and the stomp, the foot-tap groove. Keeps it moving.
- **Light kick + clap (or hand-percussion)** — the punch under it all.
- *(Optional) a simple bass* for body. No synths, no strings — keep it rootsy and human.

## STRUCTURE (~45–60s, loop-friendly) — the peaks and lows
```
0:00–0:04  THE RIFF in — guitar hook, instant identity
0:04–0:12  BUILD — tambourine + light kick join, energy rising
0:12–0:20  ★ PEAK 1 — harmonica lead over the full groove (the high)
0:20–0:27  ▽ DROP — strip back to guitar + tambourine, a breath (the low)
0:27–0:40  ★★ PEAK 2 — everything in, the big punchy chorus-feel (the highest point)
0:40–0:48  RESOLVE — back to the riff, land clean so it loops to 0:00 seamlessly
```
The two peaks + the drop between = the "higher peaks and lows" punch you wanted.

## TECH NOTES (so it scores clean)
- **Tempo:** upbeat, ~120–130 BPM. **Key:** bright/major (hopeful).
- **Loop point:** END on the riff / a clean downbeat so the last note flows back into the first.
- **Length:** 45–60s is plenty — I loop it seamlessly under any runtime.
- **Export:** highest quality (WAV or 320k MP3). Drop it in the repo and tell me the path.
