## The Scene

Picture him.

Somewhere in Italy. Could be outside Rome. Could be nowhere. A motel that's seen better days - neon sign half-lit, parking lot empty except for his beat-up Fiat.

But inside? A room full of vinyl. Real records. Stones, Fender, Kasabian, U2, Beatles. Decades of rock stacked against water-stained walls.

**He doesn't do this for money.**

The station probably pays him nothing. Maybe gas money. Maybe just lets him use the booth because nobody else wants the 10pm-2am slot.

---

## The Truth

He's got ONE listener in a camper in Valderice. One guy with a CBDer and moka who actually HEARS it.

And that's enough.

**That's the whole strategy.**

## Just Ice Juice

- **Just** = only, simple, pure
- **Ice** = cool, preserved, survives
- **Juice** = energy, fuel, the good stuff

The DJ doesn't need 10 million streams. He needs ONE person at 11pm who feels "Shame Shame Shame" hit different. Who hears "Beautiful Day" and looks up at the Sicilian sky.

**Quality of connection > quantity of reach.**
