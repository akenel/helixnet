## 4. REGULATORY FRAME (one paragraph, since detail lives elsewhere)

CBD/hemp products **below 1% THC** are legal to produce, sell and buy across all language regions (the **CH-specific 1% line** — vs the EU's 0.2–0.3%). **≥1% THC = prohibited narcotic**, except medical (prescribable without BAG sign-off since Aug 2022). Eight federal **pilot trials** are live (above). A broader legalization reform is only in **draft consultation** as of 2026 — not enacted. *The 1% line is the exact boundary a Swiss-native POS/inventory product should encode.* See [[banco-members-club-smoking-legal]] and [[banco-cafe-vat-spec]] for the deeper compliance picture.

## 5. PAIN POINTS → THE PACKAGE (the walk-in pitch)

What these small retailers actually struggle with, and which arm of the package answers it:

| Pain point (theirs) | Package answer (ours) |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| **Swiss VAT is messy** — dine-in 8.1% vs takeaway 2.6%, alcohol/tobacco always 8.1%; ≥20-seat cafés can't use flat-rate | **Banco / Helix POS** — per-line VAT split, Swiss franc, Z-report. *No US POS does this.* CBD360's bar = a live demo site. |
| **Inventory of unmarked goods** — loose flower, no barcodes, hand-counts | **Banco sell-to-seed catalog + camera scan + lazy capture** — catalog accretes as you sell; scan or photo, don't pre-load ([[banco-inventory-roadmap]]). |
| **Marketing is restricted** — can't advertise cannabis freely | **ISOTTO print + La Piazza** — compliant in-store cards, tent cards, QR-to-reviews; a marketplace presence that isn't paid ads. |
| **Payments / margins thin** | One Swiss-native stack instead of 3 foreign subscriptions; per-cashier drawer = shrink control. |
| **No community / loyalty / repeat** | **La Piazza** (community destination) + Banco loyalty credits ("two ladders, one currency") + the **member community** below. |
| **They're isolated** — no shared knowledge | **The Swiss CBD knowledge base / member community** (§6). |

**The one-line walk-in:** *"I build a Swiss-native till that handles your 8.1/2.6 VAT and your loose-flower inventory, prints your in-store cards, puts you on a community marketplace, and gives your customers a club to join — one Swiss stack instead of four foreign apps."*

**Order of doors at each shop:** lead with the **pain they feel today** (VAT or inventory), show **Banco**, then widen to La Piazza + print + community. Leave a **sticker** (the community on-ramp) regardless of whether they buy the POS.

The user runs this himself. Every door visited = a sticker = a join. The goal is a **Swiss CBD/head-shop knowledge base & member community** where shoppers and shop-people help each other — recommendations, where-to-find, grow tips, compliance Q&A.
