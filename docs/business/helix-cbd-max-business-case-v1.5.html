48-Hour Pivot Plan (If CBD Banned)

Key insight: If flower is banned, oil and vapes go too — they all contain CBD. The ban targets the cannabinoid, not just the form.

If BannedWe Still Have
CBD flower onlyVapes, oils, seeds, accessories, drinks
ALL CBD productsSeeds (0% THC), accessories, tourist line, drinks
Everything cannabis-relatedTourist souvenirs, beer, drinks, snacks
The Paraquat Lesson: In the late 1970s, US-backed paraquat spraying wiped out Mexican cannabis overnight. Colombian Gold filled the void within weeks. Markets shift fast — but headshops that sold papers, pipes, and accessories survived because they were more than one thing.

We're not building a "CBD vending machine." We're building a 24/7 headshop with tourist souvenirs that happens to sell CBD when legal.

  1. Remove banned products within 48 hours
  2. Expand seeds, accessories, Trapani tourist line
  3. Increase beer/drinks slots
  4. Communicate pivot to customers
  5. Continue operations — machine never goes dark

Che Maria dies if CBD banned. We pivot and keep selling Trapani grinders to Egadi ferry tourists.
