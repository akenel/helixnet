## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**What:** Premium handcrafted postcard experience kits sold to tourists at scenic locations across Sicily.

**How:** Mobile vendors ("Foo Fighters") on bikes/scooters approach tour buses and tourist hotspots, offering full-service: tourist picks a design, writes the address, vendor handles everything else - folding, adding local herbs and sand, wax sealing, stamping, mailing. The tourist walks away with nothing to carry. The postcard arrives home before they do.

**Why it works:**
- Tourists want authentic, memorable souvenirs - not factory-made junk
- They have 10 minutes before the bus leaves - no time to find a post office
- A personal connection with a local is THE experience
- Low startup cost (€20) means anyone can become a Foo Fighter
- Can't be copied by factories - the PERSON is the product

**Revenue potential:** €5-15 per tourist × 60 tourists per bus = €300-900 per bus stop

## THE PROBLEM

1. **Tourists** want to send something real home - not another fridge magnet made in China
2. **Tourists** don't have time to find post offices, buy stamps, figure out international mail
3. **Locals** need income - jobs are scarce, the system is rigged against small operators
4. **Existing souvenirs** are generic, forgettable, mass-produced garbage

## THE SOLUTION: UFA FOO FIGHTERS

A network of independent mobile vendors selling premium postcard experience kits.

### The Foo Fighter
- Local person on a bike/scooter
- Knows the scenic spots, knows the bus drivers by name
- Carries everything needed in a backpack/basket
- Provides FULL SERVICE - tourist does nothing but pick and pay

### The Pitch (10 seconds)
> "Pick your postcard - WWIII design or Pink? Write the address here. Purple wax seal? Good choice. I'll mail it for you today. €8. Grazie!"

### The Experience
1. Tourist picks postcard design from selection
2. Writes recipient address on clipboard
3. Picks wax seal color
4. Pays cash
5. **DONE** - Foo Fighter handles:
   - Folding the box
   - Adding local herbs (wild rosemary, oregano)
   - Adding sand/shell/pebble from local beach
   - Melting wax, stamping seal
   - Applying international stamp
   - Dropping at post office end of day

## PRODUCT LINE

### 1. Postcard (Core Product)
- **Size:** 140mm × 95mm (fits in box)

- **Design series:** Sicily Series No. 001, 002, etc.
- **Special editions:** WWIII, PINK, Bauhaus, Banksy tribute designs

### 2. Experience Box
- **Size:** 148mm × 100mm base, 10mm walls (thin for postal rates)
- **Contains:** Postcard, herb sachet, sand/shell, personal note

## OPERATIONS

### Daily Workflow (Foo Fighter)
1. **Morning:** Check inventory, pack backpack
2. **Midday:** Position at tourist hotspots (viewpoints, landmarks, bus stops)
3. **Afternoon:** Work the crowds, full service sales
4. **Evening:** Drop mail bag at post office, restock

### Production Workflow
1. **Design:** Create new postcard designs (HTML templates)
2. **Print:** Local copy shop, bulk orders for better rates
3. **Cut:** Simple cutter, or pay shop to cut
4. **Assemble:** Foo Fighters pre-fold boxes during slow times
5. **Source:** Collect herbs, sand, shells from local nature (FREE)

### Quality Control
- Every box hand-assembled
- Every seal hand-stamped
- Personal touch = premium feel
- Reject anything that looks cheap
