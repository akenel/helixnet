What this is: the one-time checklist to put Corrado's first 12 pieces on La Piazza and sell them hands-off. Tick each step as it's done; tap 🎤 to leave a voice note or 📋 to drop a photo. When the drop is live, Export and send the .txt back to the group.
DO FIRST Sylvie Corrado You (Angel) La Piazza (automatic)
Money never touches La Piazza. Buyers pay Corrado directly (bank / Revolut / cash). The app routes the offer and tracks sold — that's it.

DO FIRST 0 · Wire up Corrado (one time)

The whole hands-off plan depends on an offer reaching Corrado's phone. Prove it before any piece goes up.

  1. Corrado has a La Piazza account, and the 12 listings will live under his account. offers route to him, not to you
  2. In his account: Telegram linked + offer notifications ON. his settings show Telegram connected
  3. Test it: make a tiny test offer on any listing → Corrado's phone pings on Telegram. a real notification lands on a real phone

Sylvie 1 · Pick the 12

  1. Choose 12 pieces that hang together — theme, colours, a set that looks gorgeous as a group. 12 picked

Sylvie 2 · Tag, name, order, story

This is the catalog copy — and the one decision per piece that matters: original or edition.

  1. Tag each piece: ORIGINAL (1 of 1) or EDITION (1 of N). most framed one-offs = Original; only tag Edition if Corrado truly wants a numbered run
  2. Name each piece + give the set its order (1→12) and each a one-line story. 12 names + an order + 12 one-liners
  3. Pick the one hero image — the piece that leads the drop. 1 hero chosen

Corrado 3 · Shoot

  1. Lay out the 12. Tripod. One straight-on photo each, one session. 12 clean straight-on shots
  2. Send all 12 to the WhatsApp group — no zip, just send them. 12 photos in the chat

You 4 · Pull the photos

  1. Save the 12 photos off WhatsApp onto the machine, named to the catalog order. 12 files, in order

You 5 · Build the 12 listings

Each one a SELL listing under Corrado's account — the "Make an Offer" button appears automatically.

  1. For each piece: photo · name · one-line story · €200 · "offers welcome" · shipping €20 registered from Trapani · pickup option. 12 listings live under Corrado
  2. Edition-tagged pieces: note the edition in the listing (e.g. "Edition of N"). edition pieces marked; originals say 1 of 1

You 6 · Print the 12 cards

The claim ticket that travels with each piece when it sells: artwork + serial + QR (→ the piece's page on La Piazza).

  1. Generate the 12 Locandina cards — ribbon reads "ORIGINAL · 1 of 1 · № 00X" or "EDITION · 1 of N", QR → the piece's page. 12 cards, each scans to its own listing
  2. Scan-check 2–3 cards with a phone. QR opens the right piece on La Piazza
  3. Set the cards aside — each travels with its piece when it sells. cards stored, matched to pieces

Corrado 7 · Approve

  1. Open the live link, look at all 12, say yes (or flag fixes). Corrado's green light

Corrado You 8 · Share

This is the whole point — the photo going out wide is free advertising to the global market.

  1. Both press Share → WhatsApp / Telegram. Corrado: Rome + art-show crowd. You: Sicily + Northern-Europe. the drop is out
  2. Bonus: Sylvie makes one photo of all 12 arranged as a board — your strongest marketing shot. the board image, posted

La Piazza Corrado 9 · A sale happens

The hands-off loop. The app does the routing; Corrado does the human bits; money stays off-platform.

  1. Buyer makes an offer → Corrado pinged on Telegram → they agree a price. offer landed, deal struck
  2. Buyer pays Corrado directly (bank / Revolut / cash). money received — off-platform
  3. Corrado signs + dates + numbers the back, adds the QR card, mails registered. piece + certificate on their way
  4. Mark the listing Sold in La Piazza. scans now show SOLD; the ledger logs the date

You 10 · Read the votes

  1. Watch what sells and at what offer price — that's your real pricing data. drop 2 gets priced off what actually moved, not opinion
