🐺 Banco Discovery Field Kit

The field man's checklist · aim for coverage, not perfection · ~50 photos is fine

1 · 📸 Shots — tick as you shoot

Each line = a few shots of that thing. Don't count — cover.
Storefront — sign, front door, window+ the street: parking, neighbours
Panorama — 2–3 wide shots of the whole inside
Behind the counter — the till area, how it's laid out
The hardware, close up — tablet/PC, card reader, scanner, printer, cash drawer
Shelves — one shot per section/categorythis is what builds his catalog
Price labels — a few close-ups of how products are tagged/priced
The back office — where he does the paperwork/books

2 · 🗣️ The interview — his words

Capture how HE says it. Short notes are fine.

3 · 📄 Data samples — the replication fuel

Photograph these. This is what I rebuild in Banco.
ONE day's closeout / cash-up — let HIM pick the day"point at any day — I'll rebuild exactly that one"
A week of sales (if he offers it)
The spreadsheet / sales book / ledger
A receiving / delivery note or two (stock coming in)

4 · 🔌 Hardware & infra — what we plug into

Is the till connected to stock/inventory? (usually NO — tick only if YES. NO = the gap Banco fills)

5 · ✅ Wrap

Left it warm — thanked him, no pressure
