## 🐺 Blueprint 1 — Angel (user #1, Patient Zero)

**North Star:** Secure a paid SAP integration contract within 30 days — by shipping **one real outward action a day** instead of disappearing into the build.

- **Gifts:** SAP PI/PO mastery · end-to-end delivery · cloud-native (Azure/iPaaS) · full-stack (FastAPI/Docker) · multilingual (EN/DE/FR)
- **Honest gaps:** no active outreach / personal-branding pipeline · limited recent freelance visibility · sparse recent portfolio
- **Assets:** LinkedIn network · Swiss Life / Nestlé track record · **La Piazza itself as a live demo** · Calendly · old-employer contacts
- **The Human Check (baseline KPIs — re-measure these):** the real win isn't tasks-done, it's *energy aimed outward, procrastination shrinking, belief rising.* The body isn't the constraint — where the energy is aimed is.
- **First cuts:** Day 1 update LinkedIn headline → Day 2 About (150 words) → Day 3 2-page portfolio → Day 4 post it → Day 5 list 10 targets → Day 6 draft outreach → Day 7 send to two.
- **Pivot rule:** outreach fails 3× → switch channel / tweak message / new targets.

## 🏛️ Blueprint 2 — La Piazza (the platform)

**North Star (90-day go-live):** **One real person, out of the dark night, coached by La Piazza to a real, told-out-loud win.** Proof it works on a human. Not features.

- **Gifts:** the factory (Bottega recipes) · real distributed compute (DO worker) · 300+ mentors Housed · the coaching/Blueprint engine (proven) · Grafana · live marketplace (33k views/mo) · POS, lockboxes, feedback/backlog/QA · the story.
- **Honest gaps:** **zero proven real users coached to a win** · distribution unproven · Legend Journey is parts not a walk · sprawl · public-box login gapped · Telegram adoption.
- **Assets:** the cast · the engines · Angel's real network (Flora, Corrado, Diego, the tribe) · story-as-marketing.

| Phase | Focus | Cuts |
|---|---|---|
| **Days 1–30** | *Make it walkable, walk it yourself* | Assemble the Legend Journey into one loop (Survey→Blueprint→task→mentor→Thursday review→Grafana) · fix the public login · **dogfood your own blueprint** · clean only what blocks the walk |
| **Days 31–60** | *First hand-held cohort* | Onboard Flora/Corrado/Diego by hand · wake the cast's DOer jobs (one/day, **don't flood**) · convert to Telegram · weekly reviews · measure KPIs |
| **Days 61–90** | *Prove one comeback* | Drive ONE member to a real win · capture it as a **story** (the rope to the next) · scale only what usage demands |

- **Pivot rule:** a member won't engage after 3 honest attempts → pivot or move your energy to who's moving. Don't burn out on the third.
- **The keel:** one cut a day · one member a day · don't flood · Thursday reviews · measure in Grafana · **resist the factory** — the lever is *real users + one comeback*, not more recipes.
