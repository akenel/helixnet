The run — walk-in to walk-out

  1. Open the app → log in → tap 🛒 New Sale.
  2. Scan a KNOWN item (one already in the catalog) → it drops into the cart. ✓ (the happy path)
  3. Scan an UNKNOWN item (a fridge thing, a cig pack) → the capture modal pops up. Three ways out:
    🔎 Adopt — search the 7,272-item reference catalog → tap the match → title + photo + barcode bound, in the cart, saved to your catalog forever.
    🔗 Link — you already stock it (no/old barcode)? Search your own catalog → Link → the barcode binds to it.
    ➕ Create fresh — not in either? Type a name + price → Create & add. 📷 Snap a photo if you like.
  4. No barcode at all (loose item)? → ➕ Create no-code item (name + price).
  5. Scan more items · scan the same one twice = qty 2 · remove a wrong line.
  6. 💳 Checkout → enter the payment → Complete.
  7. 🧾 Receipt shows the items + the “Come find us on La Piazza” QR.
  8. 📱 Scan that QR with the phone camera → it lands on La Piazza. (the on-ramp — the thing we just built)
  9. (optional) 👤 Members → enroll a buyer by name → points land on the sale.
