1 · AXIUM DX8000 — a card sale that approves the payoff

The fixed counter terminal (the one we'll integrate first). Watch the amount reach it and the receipt come back.

  1. Leave the outcome on ✓ Approve. Click Charge CHF 42.50 under the AXIUM DX8000. the amount POPS onto its screen ("Betrag · CHF 42.50")
  2. The screen prompts "Karte oder TWINT auflegen" with a pulsing contactless ring. Tap KARTE (or let it auto-tap). it shows "Verarbeitung…", then green GENEHMIGT ✓
  3. Look at the till receipt (left). turns green "✓ BEZAHLT", shows Method, Terminal = AXIUM DX8000, TID 25409030, and a Txn ref like 25409030-000001

2 · TWINT — the Swiss must-have non-negotiable

TWINT is the reason we chose Worldline over SumUp. It must ride the same integrated flow as a card.

  1. Charge again on either terminal, and this time tap TWINT (the teal button) when prompted. approves the same way — GENEHMIGT ✓
  2. Check the receipt's Method line. reads TWINT — the method is recorded, not just "card"

3 · Move/5000 — the handheld (fallback terminal) both terminals

Felix's second terminal — the one that stays live as backup. It must work the same.

  1. Charge on the Move/5000 (the one with the keypad). same flow — amount pops, taps, approves
  2. Check the receipt's TID. reads 25145450 (Move/5000's own TID) and the txn ref starts 25145450-…

4 · Decline — the customer's card fails the safety net

A refused card must NOT complete the sale — the cart is kept so the cashier can retry or take cash.

  1. Switch the outcome to ✕ Decline, then Charge. the terminal shows red ABGELEHNT, "Bitte erneut versuchen"
  2. Look at the receipt. red "✕ ABGELEHNT · no charge" + "Cart is preserved — cashier can retry the card or take cash"
