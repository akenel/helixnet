1 · Search at scale ~300 real items now

Built for a thousands-item supplier catalog: fuzzy, paginated, filterable, with photos.

  1. New Sale → 🔍 Search → type a broad term (e.g. vape, cbd, liquid). results appear with product photos + "Showing X of N matches" (N is the real total)
  2. Hit "Show more". the next page appends — real pagination, not capped at 20
  3. Use the category dropdown (e.g. Vaporizers, CBD). results narrow to that category; count updates
  4. Try a typo (e.g. vapr). fuzzy search still finds the right items

2 · Product detail tap for the big picture

Tap a result to see a large photo + specs before adding — like a proper product page.

  1. Tap a product row (photo or name). a modal opens: large photo left, specs right (SKU, category, on-hand, supplier) + description
  2. Hit Add to Cart in the modal. it joins the cart; modal closes
  3. Open another, then click outside / ✕. closes cleanly, no dead-end

3 · Discount — no lost cent the rounding fix

A % discount across several items used to leave the receipt total a cent under the till total. Now they match exactly.

  1. Add 4–6 items. In the cart, apply a discount (a % button, or "Agreed Final Total"). Subtotal, Discount, TOTAL shown; note the TOTAL
  2. Checkout → confirm → open the receipt. receipt TOTAL == the till TOTAL (to the cent)
  3. On the receipt: Subtotal − Discount = TOTAL reconciles. the discount line is itemized; numbers add up

4 · Cash, change & a free treat always available · free

Cash handling is honest (no underpaid sale, no false "short"), and treats are genuinely on the house.

  1. Pay cash; tap a low amount that's under the total. red "CHF x short"; Confirm is disabled
  2. Tap EXACT (or a high note). Confirm enables; change is right (EXACT → 0.00, no −0.00)
  3. 🍭 Give a Treat → pick one. added FREE; the TOTAL does NOT change
  4. Complete → receipt. treat shows "🎁 on the house" at CHF 0.00; total unchanged

5 · Receipt — clean & one page print check

  1. Receipt shows your name as Cashier (felix), real product names, incl. VAT (8.1%). not "Cashier"/"Product"
  2. 🖨️ Print → Save as PDF. one page (no 3-4 page spill, no blank trailing page); filename datetime-stamped
