1 · Find her — search works

1 Search cynthia in the catalog search box. Her 7 balms appear (was broken — supplier name wasn't searched).
2 Search mama, then salbe, then MC-. Each finds her set (supplier / German word / SKU all hit).
3 Eyeball the 7 tiles: names, prices, her round colour label as the photo. Lavendel CHF 30, hemp/head/joint/warmth CHF 35/30, each its own colour + real label image.

2 · Read in any language

4 Open Lavendel → the detail modal. Find the 4 flags by the Description (🇩🇪 🇬🇧 🇫🇷 🇮🇹). Flag row is there; tapping a flag re-reads the description in that language, regardless of login language.
5 Tap 🇬🇧 English, then 🇩🇪 German. EN = the rich "juiced" story (olive oil, coconut, beeswax, true lavender, 30 ml, made with love). DE = Cynthia's own words, not robot German.
6 Tap 🇫🇷 and 🇮🇹. Both read cleanly; a small 🤖 "translated" mark is honest about the auto ones.

3 · The postcard — the heart of it

7 The big one: in the detail modal tap 📇 Postcard. A full-page card opens: hero label, name, "Handgemacht · Mama Cynthia", the juiced story, price in the product's colour, a QR, a serial "Nr. …", brand "Artemis Store · Banco".
8 Check the colour: lavender card = purple. Open a couple more (hemp = blue, warmth = red). Each card is themed to that product's own colour. No white space gaps — the card reads full.
9 Use the card's own DE·EN·FR·IT toolbar (top of the card). Card text + share message switch language. Anyone reads it in their tongue.
10 Scan the QR with your phone. It opens this same card on your phone (the sandbox URL). This is the shelf-label promise: scan → story in any language.

4 · Share — for a non-techy maker

11 On your phone, open a postcard and tap 📤 Share. The phone's native share sheet opens (WhatsApp, SMS, email — everything). No app to learn.
12 SMS the card to yourself at 079 828 28 41 — the "send it to Cynthia" moment. Text arrives with the card link + "show this card at checkout for a deal"; tapping opens the card.
13 On desktop (no native share), tap Share. Falls back to explicit WhatsApp / SMS / Email / Copy buttons — nothing dead-ends.

5 · 4-up print sheet (the Banksy beat)

14 On a postcard, tap 🖨️ 4-up. One A4 page, 2×2 = four cards (Format C: 3 mm frame, 6 mm gap, 99×142.5 mm).
15 Check the serials under each QR. Banksy serials …-01/04 … 04/04 — same run code, four numbered cards (can't be photocopied blind).
16 Print preview the sheet (turn ON "background graphics"). Exactly 1 A4 page, cut ticks at the edges, one ⬍ + one ⬌ cut = 4 clean cards. No blank 2nd page.

6 · Nothing else broke

17 Do a normal sale of any product (scan/add → checkout). Checkout works as before; the till is unaffected by any of the above.
18 Open a non-Cynthia product's postcard (any Artemis item). Postcard maker works generically — this is a Felix feature for the whole catalog, not just her.
