1 · What a Swiss delivery slip looks like (what to expect)

A Swiss Lieferschein (FR bon de livraison, IT bolla di consegna) travels with the goods and typically carries:

  • Supplier name + address + UID (CHE-###.###.### MWST)
  • Lieferschein-Nr. (the slip number) and Datum (date)
  • Lieferadresse — your shop
  • A line table: Pos · Menge · Art.-Nr. · Bezeichnung · (sometimes Preis)
  • Often a Total + a reservation-of-title line — the AI is told to skip totals/VAT/shipping

Real suppliers you'll see: Tamar Trade (behind Artemis), FourTwenty, Near Dark, Hemag. Some show prices, some don't — both are fine.

5 · Round 2 — edge cases (after the happy flow feels good)

Once this is smooth we build slips that break things on purpose:

  • Not in catalogue — an item we don't stock → should offer ＋ New item
  • Brand-new maker item — no barcode → new item + minted ECO/ITEM code
  • Messy / handwritten slip, a PDF, a phone photo at an angle
  • No prices on the slip · mixed languages · a long 15-line slip
  • Wrong supplier picked vs the slip's supplier — does it warn / auto-suggest?
