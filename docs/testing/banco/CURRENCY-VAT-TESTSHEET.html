The lesson driving this sheet: this whole feature kept looking right on screen while being wrong underneath — rates that didn't save, VAT that displayed 22.1% but recorded 8.1%. So the golden rule here: CHECK THE RECEIPT, not just the checkout. The receipt (and the drawer) show what was actually recorded — that's the truth.

Two logins: felix (admin — sets rates) and pam (cashier). Sandbox is the Roma / EUR shop, standard VAT 22.1%. SANDBOX only hard-refresh after a change (SW cache)

0 · Log in felix + pam

Open Banco (sandbox) ↗ https://sandbox-banco.lapiazza.app/pos
  1. Sign in as felix (admin). dashboard, role admin

1 · Accepted rates persist the one that didn't save

The bug: rates vanished on reload (an unmapped DB column). Now they stick.

Settings → Tax ↗
  1. As felix, Settings → Tax → 💱 Accepted currencies & rates. Add/adjust a currency (e.g. CHF), Save. saves, no error
  2. Reload the page (Ctrl-Shift-R). the rate is STILL there — it persisted
  3. The Add-currency dropdown does NOT list the shop's home currency (EUR). only foreign currencies are addable

2 · VAT comes from settings — CHECK THE RECEIPT the 8.1 vs 22.1 bug

The nasty one: the checkout showed 22.1% but the recorded receipt said 8.1%. The receipt is truth.

New Sale ↗
  1. Confirm Settings → Tax shows standard VAT = 22.1% (A / standard / default). 22.1 in the table
  2. Ring a €100 sale (e.g. 2 × €50), go to checkout. "incl. VAT (22.1%): €18.10" — label AND amount both 22.1%
  3. Tap the "incl. VAT" line → the breakdown tooltip. Net €81.90 · VAT (22.1%) €18.10 · Total €100 · "prices include VAT" note
  4. Complete the sale, open the RECEIPT. receipt reads "VAT A = 22.1%" and "incl. VAT: €18.10" — NOT 8.1% / €7.49

3 · Take foreign cash — change in home, receipt honest Block 1

Customer pays foreign cash; you give change in EUR; the sale is recorded in EUR + stamped.

  1. Open your drawer (My Drawer → Open Shift), then ring a €100 sale → Checkout → Cash. the currency picker appears: EUR + your foreign currencies
  2. Tap CHF. "collect ≈ CHF …" hint; the Amount received field switches to CHF
  3. Enter a CHF amount over the total (e.g. CHF 100 at rate 1.08). change shows in EUR (100×1.08 − 100 = €8.00)
  4. Complete → open the RECEIPT. "Paid in cash: CHF 100.00 @ 1.08" · Amount Tendered €108.00 · Change €8.00 · TOTAL/VAT in EUR

4 · Only the configured currencies show per-shop

  1. The cash picker offers EXACTLY the currencies in your Settings table — no others. e.g. only CHF/USD if that's what's set
  2. (Optional) Remove one currency in Settings, save, hard-refresh, back to checkout. that currency is gone from the picker

5 · Drawer close sees the foreign cash Block 2

My Drawer ↗
  1. After the foreign-cash sale, go to close the drawer. a "💱 Foreign cash in the drawer" block lists CHF 100.00 ≈ €108.00 (count separately)
  2. The Expected-in-drawer figure includes that foreign cash's home value. expected reflects the sale; you count home + foreign notes
