0 · Log in to sandbox

The till screen where a sale (and a new item) begins.

Open Sandbox · New Sale ↗ https://sandbox-banco.lapiazza.app/pos/scan
  1. Log in (as Pam / cashier) and land on New Sale. you see "Find Product" + an empty cart, top bar says SBX

1 · Scan the box — the miss start here

In sandbox this item doesn't exist yet, so scanning it should NOT dead-end — it should hand you a way forward.

  1. Steady the gun, scan 42425700 into the barcode field. the code reads first try (or with one steady re-aim)
  2. It's not found. a "Not on file — find it first" panel appears with clear choices — never a dead end

2 · One photo — and it STAYS your #1 pain

The whole fight: you take a picture, do all the work, and the image is gone at the end. Goal — one photo, used everywhere, never asked twice.

  1. Tap 📸 Snap & fill (phone) or Webcam (laptop) and take ONE photo of the box. it accepts the shot and starts reading it — no "try again"
  2. It drafts the fields from your photo. name / category / a price guess come back filled in
  3. Look for your photo. After the draft, is the picture you just took still attached to the form? YES — the same shot is queued as the product image; you are NOT asked to photograph again

3 · Or search by name

Sometimes typing the name is faster than the camera. (Sandbox is thin on data, so it may find nothing — that's fine, just note it.)

  1. On the miss panel tap "or search by name instead", type gizeh king size. a name search runs — results (if any) show size + picture, or an honest "nothing here yet"

4 · Add it here — the barcode carries through no re-typing

You already scanned the code once. You should never have to read or type it again.

  1. Tap "Not found — add it here". the create form opens with 42425700 ALREADY in the barcode field
  2. Fill the name (e.g. Gizeh King Size Slim Extra Fine Black 34) and price 2.00. quick, no fighting the keyboard; category can stay/auto
  3. Save. it saves without kicking you out or throwing an error

5 · The payoff — the image actually SHOWS ticket #2 / #4

Your two backlog tickets: "image in catalog but not when you scan out" / "new product image not showing." This is the check that proves it.

  1. Scan 42425700 again at the till. it now RINGS UP your new GIZEH item
  2. Look at it in the cart / on screen. the photo you took SHOWS — no blank box, no broken image
  3. Open it in the Catalog too. same picture shows there as well — catalog AND scan agree

6 · Where it fought you the clunk log

The honest field diary. This is what turns into the rebuild.

  1. Did you have to switch devices (laptop → phone) to get a photo? the goal: one device does the whole thing
  2. Did a stray tap ever lose your work / kick you out? the goal: it warns you, nothing is lost
  3. Total photos + scans you took of this ONE item: write the number. the goal: 1 scan + 1 photo, full stop
