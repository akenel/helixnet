0 · Open the till & log in setup

Fresh window, Banco front door, real login.

Open Banco POS ↗ staging-banco.lapiazza.app/pos · Incognito
Users: pam / helix_pass (cashier — the one who enrolls) or felix / helix_pass (admin).
  1. Incognito → Banco POS → Login. dashboard loads, "Welcome, …", no error
  2. On the dashboard there's a 👤 Members card. it's there (was URL-only before); tap it → the Members page opens

1 · Sign up a member 10 seconds · 18+ tick

"Are you a member? Want to be — discount today?" Pam adds them in seconds. No email or password needed — just a name (Instagram is the gold identifier).

Open Members ↗
  1. Search a name that won't exist (e.g. TEST-charlie). "not found" → an Add New button appears
  2. Open the form. Notice the ✓ 18 or older box and the optional "send me offers" box. Create is disabled until you tick 18+
  3. Fill Handle (TEST-charlie) + Instagram (e.g. charlie420), tick 18+, leave offers unticked. Create. "Welcome … credits awarded"; the member appears with a tier badge
  4. Try to create one without ticking 18+. blocked — you can't enroll without the age tick

2 · Find a member & read their card tier · credits

Next time they come in, look them up — see who they are, their tier, and their points.

  1. Search the member by handle, Instagram, or real name. card shows: tier badge, discount %, credits, visits, lifetime spend (a real CHF amount, not "undefined") — no SEEDLING/KBs, no console errors
  2. Check Recent members below the search. recently-seen members listed; tap one → it loads them

3 · Put them on a sale the member banner

A member rides along with the sale — the cashier sees who's buying.

  1. On the member's card, hit ✅ Use for Checkout. you land on New Sale / Scan
  2. Ring up a couple of items → Checkout. a blue member banner at the top shows their handle · tier · points
  3. (Optional) hit remove on the banner. the member detaches; "Add a member →" link shows. Re-attach by going back through Members.

4 · Points land + Bronze pays full the wire-up

A new member is Bronze — no standing discount yet, but every CHF earns a point. (Spend grows the tier; see §5.)

  1. With the Bronze member attached, note the TOTAL. no "Member discount" line for Bronze — they pay full this time
  2. Complete the sale (card is simplest). receipt as normal; you stay logged in
  3. Go back to Members → search the same member. credits went UP (≈ 1 per CHF) and lifetime spend increased

5 · The tier climb Bronze → Silver → Gold

Spend grows the tier automatically: Silver at CHF 500 (+5%), Gold at CHF 2000 (+10%). Conservative on purpose — Felix keeps his shirt.

  1. Ring a big sale for the member to cross CHF 500 (use a pricey item, or "Agreed Final Total"). Complete it. no discount on THIS sale (they were Bronze when it rang)
  2. Re-search the member. tier is now Silver, discount shows 5%
  3. Start a new sale with that Silver member → Checkout. a "Member discount (Silver 5%)" line now shows; the TOTAL is 5% lower
  4. (Optional) push past CHF 2000 lifetime → re-search. tier Gold, 10% member discount on the next sale

6 · Feel & polish

  1. The whole loop feels natural — Members ⇄ card ⇄ Use for Checkout ⇄ sale ⇄ points. no dead-ends, nothing confusing for Pam
  2. Nothing visually broken (banner, badges, CHF, the % discount line). clean and trustworthy
