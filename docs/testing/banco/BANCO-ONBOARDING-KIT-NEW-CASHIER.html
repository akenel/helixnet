ONBOARD-KIT-01
👋 Trainer: hand over the phone + this sheet, say “start at step 1.” Then hands in your pockets. Don't touch, don't explain — let them drive. Every time they get stuck is something for us to fix, not for you to rescue. Watch their thumbs, not the clock.

Welcome. Let's get you on the till.

📱 Get your phone out. Work top to bottom. Read a step, do it, then tick the box. Nobody's timing you, and you can't break anything. You do not need to know any prices — the till knows them. Your job is just to point, scan, and press buttons.

1 · Find your way in

1
Open the browser and go to banco.lapiazza.app.You see a wolf and a Login button.
2
Tap Login and sign in with the account Felix gave you.You land on the till — a big New Sale button.
Stuck? If it looks frozen, pull the screen down to refresh once, then try again.

2 · Ring your first sale

1
Tap New Sale.An empty basket (cart).
2
Tap the 📷 camera, point it at a product's barcode.The item drops into the basket with its price. You typed nothing — the till knew the price.
Got the scanner gun? Just point it and pull the trigger instead — same result.
3
Buying more than one? Tap the quantity number and type it — say 15.The total updates. Don't scan it 15 times — just type 15.
4
Tap Checkout. Turn the screen so the customer sees the total.They see the number and agree — no argument, just like the calculator.
5
Tap how they're paying — Cash, Visa, or TWINT. For cash, type what they hand you.For cash it shows the change to give back. Sale done — a receipt number appears.
🎉 That's a full sale — and you didn't know a single price.

3 · The bulk price (you do nothing)

1
Ask Felix for an item with a bulk deal (like papers). Scan it, then set the quantity to 3, then 5.Watch the price per item drop by itself.
💡 When someone buys a few, the cheaper price kicks in automatically. You never work it out — you just set the number.

4 · When the scan won't work (never stuck)

1
Barcode won't scan? Tap Search and type part of the name.Matches appear — tap the right one.
2
Still nothing? Every shelf label has a short code printed on it. Type that in the barcode box.It finds the item.
3
A weird one-off with no code? Tap 🆕 New item, type a name and the price Felix tells you.It rings like anything else.
💡 There is always a way to ring something. You are never stuck at the counter.

5 · Add a new product from a photo

1
New stock the till doesn't know yet? Tap 🆕 New item → tap the camera → photograph the product.The till reads the photo and looks for a match.
2
Pick the match (or the supplier's version for a nicer photo). Check the price. Save.It's now in the till for next time.
Suppliers: the till can look up products from the shop's suppliers and shows where each came from. Ask Felix which suppliers matter for your shelves.

6 · Members & discounts (know your limit)

1
A regular with a member card? At checkout tap “Add a member →” and find them.Their discount shows and comes off by itself. They always get it.
2
Want to knock a little extra off? Type a discount %.You can go up to your limit. Past it, the screen says “ask a manager” — that's normal, not a mistake.
💡 The member's discount is automatic. Your own extra discount has a limit Felix sets. And the till will never let a price go below zero — you can't accidentally give money away.

7 · End of the day

1
At close, open the day's summary.Today's totals: cash, Visa, TWINT, and the grand total — added up for you.
💡 That's the one line Felix puts into his accounting. The till hands you the exact numbers — no adding up by hand.

🐺 You can run the counter

Ticked every box above? Then you can:

  • Ring a sale by scanning — without knowing prices
  • Type a quantity instead of scanning 15 times
  • Take cash, Visa or TWINT and give the right change
  • Ring anything even when the barcode won't scan
  • Add a new product from a photo
  • Attach a member and stay inside your discount limit
  • Read off the day's totals for Felix
