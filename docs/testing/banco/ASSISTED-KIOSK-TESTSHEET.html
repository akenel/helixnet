The story we're testing: a customer's item won't scan. Pam (logged in) opens the kiosk, scans it, sees it's not in the system — and instead of "ask staff", she creates it right there: snaps a photo, names it, prices it, and it's live & sellable. Born-once, at the counter, in front of the customer.

How to use: log in as pam first, then work top to bottom. Tick each check, jot anything odd in the note box under each step, and hit Export report at the end to send it back to Tigs. Ticks & notes auto-save in this browser. SANDBOX only — no prod machine-green ≠ human-green — this is your sign-off

Best on Chrome. The photo step uses the device camera (tablet/phone) or a file pick (laptop).

0 · Log in as Pam cashier

The whole point is that the newest cashier can do this herself. Log in as pam (pos-cashier), not a manager.

Open Banco (sandbox) ↗ https://sandbox-banco.lapiazza.app/pos
  1. Sign in as pam and land on the dashboard. "Welcome, pam" · role shows pos-cashier
  2. The 🖥️ Guest Kiosk card is on Pam's dashboard. the card is there for a cashier (not managers-only anymore)

1 · The guest kiosk still has NO powers the safety rule

Powers must never leak onto a guest device. Open the kiosk the way a GUEST would — cold, not from Pam's dashboard. Easiest: a fresh Incognito window (no login there).

Open the kiosk as a guest ↗ https://sandbox-banco.lapiazza.app/pos/kiosk
  1. In Incognito, open the kiosk URL. plain guest kiosk — NO "👑 Assisted" badge top-left
  2. Search a made-up name/number that isn't stocked. the "🤔 we couldn't find it — ask our team" screen, with NO ➕ Create button
  3. Now add ?staff=1 to the URL in that same Incognito (still no login). STILL a plain guest — the flag alone gives nothing without a real login

2 · Pam launches the assisted kiosk M1

Back in your normal (logged-in-as-pam) window. Launch the kiosk from the dashboard card — it should know it's her.

Pam's dashboard ↗
  1. Tap the 🖥️ Guest Kiosk card. the kiosk opens in a new tab
  2. Look top-left. a gold 👑 Assisted · pam badge — the kiosk knows who launched it
  3. Scan (gun) or type a barcode that ISN'T in the catalog, e.g. a random number. the not-found screen — and now a big amber ➕ Create this product button

3 · Create the product right there M2 — the money shot

This is Angel's story: "it's not in the system — I can fix that." No leaving the screen.

  1. Tap ➕ Create this product. a "New product" form opens; the Barcode field is prefilled with what you just scanned
  2. Tap the 📷 photo box. the camera opens (tablet/phone) or a file picker (laptop)
  3. Take/pick a photo. a thumbnail of it fills the photo box
  4. Leave the Name empty, tap Create & sell. it's blocked — a small red hint, nothing created
  5. Type a Name, set Price to 0 (or blank), tap Create. still blocked — price must be > 0
  6. Put in a real Name + Price (your "ten boxes, deal" price), tap Create & sell. it creates and lands you on the NEW product's normal kiosk page — your photo + your price showing
  7. On that product page, tap Add to basket (+). the basket count goes up — it's a real, sellable product

4 · It's a REAL product, not a phantom integrity

The create must go through the same funnel as the till (canonical category, 18+ class) — a real catalog row, findable, with the barcode wired.

Product Catalog ↗
  1. In Product Catalog (or New Sale search), search the name you created. it's there — ONE product, with your photo and price
  2. Scan/enter the SAME barcode again (kiosk or till). now it's FOUND — no longer a miss (born once → hits forever)
  3. Check its category. a sensible canonical category (or "Unsorted") — never blank/garbage

5 · Robustness & no leaks try to break it

The little things that bite: cancels, reloads, and the guest boundary holding under pressure.

  1. Open Create, then tap Cancel. back to the kiosk cleanly — nothing half-created
  2. Reload the assisted kiosk tab (still logged in as pam). the 👑 badge is still there — it stays Pam's kiosk
  3. Back in Incognito (guest), re-open the kiosk and hit a miss. STILL no Create button — the guest boundary holds
  4. Create a product with NO photo (skip the camera). still creates — the 📦 placeholder shows, photo is optional

  1. M1 + M2 good on sandbox? — tick if the assisted kiosk does the job for Pam.
  2. Ship to prod? — tick ONLY if you want Tigs to ladder M1+M2 to Felix's live shop.
