3 · 18+ toggle on the quick-add 💰 pam compliance

A cashier can flag a new item 18+ on the spot — and that must actually gate the sale, not just look pretty.

Open New Sale ↗
  1. In "New item on the fly", there's a big 18+ toggle between price and photo, default OFF. clear 🔞/✅ toggle, starts OFF
  2. Name + price, flip 18+ ON (🔞), Create & add to cart, go to checkout & try to pay. the sale is BLOCKED — asks to verify 18+ / attach an of-age member
  3. Verify age (attest) → pay. now it completes; receipt shows the 🔞 line
  4. Ring a normal item with 18+ OFF. no age gate — sells straight through
