# Banco — Enrichment Demo: Voice-Over Script & Card Lines

*Companion to `BANCO-ENRICHMENT-DEMO.html`. Desktop demo. Francs. Hero prop: **SWEED Royal
Kush 5g, CHF 23.90, THC 0.8% / CBD 13.7%**, pulled from the Tamar webshop (artemisluzern.ch).*

**How to use:** **CARD** = on-screen title (big text, ~2s). **VO** = what you say —
conversational, you're talking to another shop owner. Record VO separately into Telegram,
one clip per beat. *(dir.)* = a delivery note, not spoken. Punchline first, then the riff.

---

## COLD OPEN (pain-first)

**CARD:** *A hot new item. No price in the till. Customer waiting.*

**VO:** "This happens in every shop, every day: the hottest new item lands, it's not in the
system yet, and the customer's standing there while the cashier hunts for a price. Watch how
that's *supposed* to go — start to sold, under a minute."

**CARD:** **BANCO** — *the hot item is never a dead end.*

---

## ACT I — the pain (📱/🖥️ the till)

### Beat 1 — Pam hunts
**CARD:** *"Royal Kush?" … not on file.*

**VO:** "Customer wants the SWEED Royal Kush — just came in this week. Pam searches the till.
Nothing. It's not in the system. The old way, this is where everybody waits."

---

## ACT II — born once, then enrich

### Beat 2 — born once
**CARD:** *Not on file? Make it. Now.*

**VO:** "Banco doesn't stall — new item, right here in the sale. And here's the part that
matters: we don't just slap in a name and guess a price. We make it *right*."

### Beat 3 — enrich from Tamar
**CARD:** *Pull the real info — from the shop's own webshop.*

**VO:** "Twenty-three francs ninety. Category, CBD flower. The real description — Hindu Kush,
earthy and pine. THC under one percent, CBD thirteen-seven — the numbers that keep you legal.
Straight off our own website. *(dir.: small beat)* Nobody's making this up."

### Beat 4 — the photo
**CARD:** *Its real picture, in seconds.*

**VO:** "And the photo — the actual product shot. Now it's not a line in a spreadsheet.
It's a *product*."

### Beat 5 — save
**CARD:** *Born once. Perfect. Forever.*

**VO:** "Save. It's in the catalogue now — searchable, scannable, photo, price, the lot.
Made once, made right, and never again."

---

## ACT III — relate & sell

### Beat 6 — the member
**CARD:** *And it's a member at the counter.*

**VO:** "This customer? She's a member. So she goes on the sale. She's not a transaction —
she's a regular, and she earns for being one."

### Beat 7 — sell
**CARD:** *Sold. She earns. Receipt prints.*

**VO:** "Ring it through. She gets her points, the receipt's got the shop's real name and
number on it, and the whole thing took less than a minute."

---

## ACT IV — the payoff

### Beat 8 — instant next time
**CARD:** *Next customer? Instant.*

**VO:** "And the next person who wants one?" *(dir.: pause — let the instant result land)*
"There it is. Name, price, picture — instant. Nobody hunts for that price ever again."

---

## OUTRO

**CARD:** **BANCO** — *a hot item, a perfect product, a happy member. Under a minute.*

**VO:** "A new item used to mean a customer left waiting. Now it means a perfect product and a
member who feels looked after — in under a minute. That's the whole difference."
