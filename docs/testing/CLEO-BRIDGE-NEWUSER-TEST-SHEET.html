#Step  ·  what should happen STAGINGPROD Match?Notes
1 ✍️ Sign up (Get Started). On the Workshop, tap Get Started → type your name + a sentence or two about what you do + a password → submit.
Expect: you land logged in, no email-verify wall, no error.
Tigs: one-motion Get Started is live on both; a few honest sentences are enough (no CV needed).
2 👑 Cleopatra greets you. Check the inbox / welcome — she references what you just told her (not a generic "hello").
Expect: a warm note naming a strength or two she read from your words.
3 💡 Cleo suggests a listing (her pick). Use the "draft a listing" suggestion / ask Cleo what to offer.
Expect: ONE concrete move — a kind (service / item / event) + a first-person offering sentence + a short why this fits you + a coaching line. If your profile was thin, she asks ONE sharpening question instead — that's also a pass (the dignity gate).
Tigs: verified both — recommend=true with a real prefill; thin profile → polite "tell me more".
4 📢 Open "Draft a Listing", pre-filled. In the 📢 Sell & Announce shelf, open Draft a Listing — kind + a sentence about the thing.
Expect: the recipe is there and the inputs are easy (you type a LITTLE).
5 🤖 AI writes the whole listing. Run it.
Expect: a real title, a full description (what / included / who / one honest trust line), tags, and a fair EUR price + unit — grounded in your words, no hype.
Tigs: e.g. "I bake sourdough" → full listing + €/loaf, no "best in town" fluff.
6 ✏️ Review & edit (you stay in charge). Change the title or nudge the price right in the result.
Expect: title / description / price / unit are editable before sending.
7 🏛️ Send to La Piazza (one button). Click Send to La Piazza (draft).
Expect: ✓ success message + a "→ View your draft" link; no error.
Tigs: verified end-to-end as mike (staging) + angel (prod). Expect ✓ both.
8 🔗 Same person on the marketplace (SSO). Open the marketplace in the same browser — you should already be you (no second login).
Expect: no re-login; your name/profile shows. One identity across both apps.
Tigs: SSO + self-relink verified on both envs.
9 📦 The draft is waiting in La Piazza. In your account / "my listings", find the draft you just sent.
Expect: the listing is there as a DRAFT (not public yet), title + description intact.
10 🖼 It has a cover image (no naked listing). Look at the draft's photo.
Expect: a default cover ("replace with your photo") — not a grey box.
Tigs: every bridge draft gets the placeholder cover automatically.
