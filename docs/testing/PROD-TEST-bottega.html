La Piazza / Bottega — Prod Test Sheet

New-user scenarios · shipped 2026-06-16 (accordion · legends search · BYOH capability window · FAQ)
Base URL: https://bottega.lapiazza.app
Tap each link (opens in a new tab). Tick D after testing on desktop and M after mobile. Start logged out / incognito to get the true new-user view.

A · Front door — brand new visitor

The first 30 seconds. No CV, one motion.

/get-started
Expect: "Welcome to La Piazza" — enter a name + a line about what you do → "Enter your Bottega".
Expect: after login you land in the workshop; a confetti welcome and your profile % shows.
Mobile: nav collapses — check the menu opens and "Get Started" is tappable.

B · Ask Cleo — the card

The host reads you and starts your card.

/compute/me  ·  /compute/concierge (Ask Cleo)
Expect: Cleopatra greets you by name, lists what she already sees, and offers to sharpen your card.

C · The Workshop — pick a recipe (NEW accordion)

The recipe catalog, now compact.

/compute/bottega
Expect: categories are collapsed accordions — "About You" open, others closed with a ▾ and a recipe count. Tap a header → it opens, the open one closes.
Then: pick a card → answer a couple of questions → get the output (you already did this — the workout plan ✓).
Mobile: headers should be easy taps; only one open at a time = little scrolling.

D · The Legends — search (NEW)

Find a master by name across all houses.

/compute/legends
Expect: a search box on top. Type "Ben" → Benjamin Franklin appears (live, as you type). Clear → back to the Houses.
Then: open a master → ask a question → get a mentor reply.
Mobile: the search keyboard should not cover results; tapping a result opens the master.

E · The Exchange — Bring Your Own Hardware (NEW)

The capability window + the no-brainer.

/compute/dashboard → Provider tab
Expect: a "Bring your own hardware — three steps" callout, and the demo node angel-laptop with green capability badges (ffmpeg · piper · puppeteer · 15GB · no GPU) and "runs: voice, captions, cards/print, video · can't: image/3D". Hover a badge → tooltip.
Badges show for nodes that reported capabilities. A node registered via the form (no probe yet) shows none — that's the per-node-enroll upgrade, still to come.

F · Help / FAQ (NEW submenu + sections)

The no-brainer explainer for the show.

/compute/faq
Expect: a grouped submenu (Using the square / Bring your own / Trust & rules). Read "Bring Your Own Hardware", "What can my machine run?", "Lend a GPU", "Can I bring my own software?".
