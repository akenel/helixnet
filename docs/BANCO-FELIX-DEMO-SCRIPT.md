# Banco — Felix Demo Script (1 page, carry this in)

**Goal:** wow him with the scanner, then turn his "does it do X?" barrage into co-design.
**Concept in one line:** *Sell-to-seed — don't load your inventory, start selling and the
catalog builds itself.*

---

## Before you walk in (60-second pre-flight)
- [ ] Fairphone on, **Chrome**, charged. On HTTPS: `https://banco.lapiazza.app/pos`.
- [ ] Logged in as a **manager/admin** (felix / `helix_pass`) — so lazy capture *persists*.
- [ ] Have 2 items in hand: one you've **already sold once** (known), one **brand-new**.
- [ ] New Sale → Barcode → 📷 confirm the camera opens. (If permission was denied before,
      re-allow camera in Chrome site settings.)

---

## The demo — 3 beats, under 2 minutes

**Beat 1 — it already knows things.**
Scan the *known* item → rings up instantly.
> "No inventory loaded. It knows this one because we sold it before."

**Beat 2 — the wow: enter once, known forever.**
Scan the *brand-new* item → prompt: name + price + **quantity** → it sells.
Then scan the *same* item again →  instant ring-up.
> "Entered once at the counter. Now it's a real product — every future scan is instant.
> No barcode loading, no inventory project. You just start selling."

**Beat 3 — it's real.**
Show the receipt; mention the item is now a saved product you can edit later.

---

## The "does it do X?" judo (the important part)
The moment he starts firing questions, **don't get defensive — hand him the roadmap**:

> "Stock top-ups when a box arrives, what-to-reorder, item editing, German — all mapped.
> You're customer #1. You tell me what's first, and that's what we build next."

Then show the **Catalog dashboard** (P1, built): edit a price, discontinue an item, add one.
> "This is you *running* the catalog — fix prices, retire items, set stock. The selling
> builds it; this keeps it clean."

---

## Roadmap at a glance (show, don't read)
- ✅ **Now:** scan + sell-to-seed + catalog edit/discontinue (live)
- ▶ **Next (you pick the order):**
  1. **Receiving** — box arrives → scan → stock up → logged (in & out)
  2. **Units** — buy by box, sell by single
  3. **Reorder report** — what's running low → what to order
  4. **German** — EN base now, DE next
  5. **Photos + auto-descriptions** — snap at the counter, AI drafts later

---

## What to say when something isn't built yet
Not *"it doesn't do that."* Instead:
> "Foundation's already in for that — it's a wiring job, not a rebuild. Where does it sit
> on your list?"

(True: cost, supplier, reorder thresholds, soft-delete, stock-movement & PO models all
already exist in the system — they just need surfacing/connecting.)

---

## After the demo
- Note the **one feature** he wants first → that's the next BL.
- If he loves it: this is customer #1 validation. Get him saying the order out loud.
- Don't over-promise dates. Promise the *order*, not the calendar.
