The AI layer — picture → identify → search → enrich

A cashier snaps a photo → the vision LLM reads the packaging (name, brand) → searches the source → pulls price + picture + description (German → English). And an autonomous overnight job enriches the thin on-the-fly items on its own — the catalogue improves while the shop sleeps. One brain, swappable (Turbo or local).

Data & integrations — don't reinvent, feed

Mozy (the 420 wholesaler): ~7,000 articles across suppliers → the catalogue seed. · Banana (accounting): we hand it the daily ~6 numbers, never rebuild it. · HR: feed the shift-clock hours to his existing process.

The business model — the real product is the service

Not a SaaS cage — a setup-and-stewardship package, per customer, on their own box. Each shop gets its own sandbox → staging → prod (a cleaned clone). We wire it all, hand over the credentials, and steward the first 3 weeks; week 4 we go live, after everyone's trained. Hands-off or hands-on — your call. Self-training via the in-app wizard + demo videos scales the high-touch start.

Market reality: a niche, not a unicorn (CHF ~80–150/mo) — the moat is the domain fit + the stewardship.
