## Used-on-Ricardo guardrails
- **Battery** is the only real risk (all-day floor use) → ask age/battery, lean newer. Phone backup covers a dud.
- **Recent enough:** Android 12+ / 2021+ (Tab A8/A9/A9+, S6 Lite 2022, S7 FE) OR a used **iPad 9/10** (ages great, Banco runs in Safari). Avoid pre-2020 / Android ≤10.
- Consider **two cheap used** (spare) — at Ricardo prices, two used beats one new.
- Search: `Samsung Galaxy Tab A9` · `Tab S6 Lite` · `Tab S7 FE` · `iPad 9`. Meet local (Beckenried).

## Charging — DON'T overspend (Angel's frugal call, correct)
- Budget tablets charge via **USB-C ~15W**. **Any cheap charger works** — no fancy fast/PD charger needed.
- **Old USB-A bricks being dumped cheap/free are FINE:** an old 5V USB-A charger + a **USB-A→USB-C cable** charges the tablet (slower ~5-10W, perfect for **overnight**). The store's got wall power all day anyway.
- Old **USB-A power banks** + a USB-C cable = free top-up on the floor if ever needed.
- The scanner gun charges on its OWN cable (separate from the tablet) — one less thing to match.
- **Net:** scrounge a USB-A brick + buy a CHF ~5 USB-A→USB-C cable, or a CHF ~10 basic USB-C charger. Done.
