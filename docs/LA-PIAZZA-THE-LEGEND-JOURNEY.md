## The flip

Every tool ever built for the out-of-work waits for *you* to know what to ask. Blank box,
upload your CV, perform your worthiness. La Piazza does the opposite: **the platform reaches
out first.** Leonardo notices the kid. He *favorites* him. He sends the first message.

The lonely don't get a better résumé. They get **seen** — by the greatest who ever lived —
and pulled into action one zero-effort tap at a time.

## The characters are DOers

The masters (Leonardo, George Clooney, Sun Tzu, and the demo crew — Sally, Johnny) are not
chatbots you visit. They are **little bots that DO** — persona-templates with a heartbeat.
- Their *voice + life* = metadata in a template (we already know everything about Leonardo).
- Their *aliveness* = background jobs ticking on the **DO** box (DigitalOcean = the DOers' home).
  A cron tick is their pulse: they keep noticing, favoriting, posting to the help board,
  offering lessons — even when nobody's watching. The world lives on its own.
- Their *actions* come back as **JSON contracts** — `{character, action, target, message, offer}`.
  Structured, logged, real. (The output-contract idea: every mandatory field guaranteed.)

## Syn Field — the data-soul

*One seal fails, check all the seals → check all the **fields**.* Syn Field is the synthesized
attributes/objects/metadata layer: it defines what makes a character a character, AND it tracks
where the user is in his story — every milestone, every gift discovered, every gap closing.
The journey IS its fields filling in.

## The journey (gamified)

1. **Profile** — CV (+ optionally a job description he's chasing) → the AI infers his aptitude.
   POC: a few reasonable assumptions. Angel's own profile is test user #1 — *the lonely guy,
   building his own lighthouse, testing it on himself first.*
2. **Match** — aptitude → his mentors. "You've got Da Vinci's DNA" / "you're Clooney charisma" /
   "great salesman, weak marketer → here's who teaches that."
3. **Activation** — the master reaches out. Asks a question tied to his profile. The kid answers
   **1, 2, or 3** — "go ahead, submit, let's do it." Zero blank-page paralysis; a hand extended.
4. **The work** — Leonardo now has a request to act on: a help-board post for the kid, a free
   lesson at his place, a raffle worth entering, a band to join.
5. **Paced & gamified** — gauged to the user's tempo. Progress = fields filling. Milestones,
   levels, a growing story.
6. **The certificate** — at 12 months: **"LP Legend."** Not a PDF. A life:
   > *"Leo and George helped me find a community and a band. I've got a new album.
   > I'm playing a concert next week — want to come see me play?"*

## The stories are the engine

The unfolding journeys get told as stories (the storyteller thread). The stories ARE the
distribution — shareable, postcard-able, Telegram-able. Every transformation is a tale that
brings the next lonely one in. **The rope. One member at a time.**

## Mechanics (it's the machine we already proved)

- Characters = persona-recipes. · Their actions = background jobs (cron on DO → brain → JSON).
- Onboarding/aptitude = a workflow recipe filling required Syn Fields.
- Help board / favorites / messages / raffles = where character-actions land.
- The brain = Ollama Turbo (or BYO). Cheap (~$20/mo). Jobs queue off to the side of prod on DO —
  *no rough waters; any rough waters, it handles fine.*

## The one honest bar

Proactive must feel like **care, not spam.** Every favorite, message, and offer is genuinely
about *that one person* and offers something *real* — a real lesson, a real next step. The
lonely smell fake from a mile off. Quality of attention over quantity of pings.
