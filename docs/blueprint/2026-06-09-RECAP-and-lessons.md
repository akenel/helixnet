## 🎓 Lessons learned (one mistake, once → into the SOP)
1. **Shorts = native PORTRAIT.** Re-shoot at a phone viewport (mobile layout, full-frame). NEVER pad landscape into 9:16 — you get a tiny unreadable strip. → in the video SOP.
2. **Publishing ≠ distribution.** At 14 subs, the win is **a real person trying it + giving feedback**, not view counts. Go **narrow + real** (Flora/Franco/Corrado), not a broad blast.
3. **Audio is the LAST layer** — score to the feel you built; upgrade the track later. Music understated (~16–20%); let the story speak (Banksy). Captions only on voice episodes.
4. **Clean slate before re-shooting** — scrub test data (regression posts, dup posts) so the camera films the truth.
5. **The story IS the test** — the shoot found + fixed real bugs, and now those stories guard prod forever.
6. **YouTube has no "replace video"** — delete + re-upload.
