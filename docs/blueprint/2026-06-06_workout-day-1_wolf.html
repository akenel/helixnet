⚠️ Shoulder rules — read first

  1. Pain-free range only. Stretch/burn = good. Sharp pinch = stop, no ego.
  2. Neutral grip (palms facing) overhead. No behind-neck, no heavy flat bench.
  3. Warm it first (broom), feed it every session (face pulls).
  4. Smart-training, not medical advice. Real injury? Get it looked at.

🔥 Warm-up · 5–8 min

Easy movement 2–3 min
March on the spot, step-ups, or the bike — get the blood moving and a light sweat going.
Broomstick shoulder pass-throughs 2 × 10
Broom wide in both hands, slowly raise it up over your head and back behind you, then return. Leonardo's measured motion.
Why: opens & warms the shoulder safely before you load it.
▶ Watch howFlora: narrow grip if tight
Band pull-aparts 2 × 15
Hold a band in front, arms straight, pull it apart across your chest, squeeze your shoulder blades.
Why: the daily "shoulder vitamin" — wakes the rotator cuff, fixes the hunch.
▶ Watch howFlora: a towel works too
Bodyweight squats + hip hinges 2×10 + 1×10
Squats: sit down & stand up, no weight. Hinges: soft knees, push hips back, hands slide down thighs.

💪 The Workout · full body

Straight sets · slow down / controlled up · rest 60–90 sec · 2 reps in the tank.

1. Goblet Squat 3 × 10
Hold one dumbbell at your chest with both hands. Sit down like into a chair, stand back up tall.
Works: legs, glutes, core. Why: staying strong getting off chairs & floors — independence itself.
▶ Watch howFlora: no weight — sit-to-stand from a chair
2. Romanian Deadlift (RDL) 3 × 10
Dumbbells in hands, soft knees, push hips BACK (not down), lower the weights down your legs till the hamstrings stretch, stand tall.
Works: hamstrings, glutes, low back. Why: the hinge that protects your back. Deadlifted yesterday — keep LIGHT.
▶ Watch howFlora: light/no weight, small range
3. One-Arm Dumbbell Row 3 × 10 / side
One hand & knee on the bench. Other hand pulls the dumbbell up to your hip — like pull-starting a lawnmower.
Works: back, lats. Why: pulling strength & posture.
▶ Watch howFlora: light, brace on a chair
4. Neutral-Grip Overhead Press 2–3 × 8
Dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing each other. Press up only as high as feels good, lower slowly.
Works: shoulders, triceps. Why: shoulder-safe overhead strength. Stop at any pinch.
▶ Watch howFlora: seated, light, tiny range
5. Face Pulls 3 × 15
Anchor a band at face height. Pull both ends toward your face, elbows high & wide, squeeze the back of your shoulders.
Works: rear shoulders + rotator cuff. Why: 👈 THE shoulder-saver. Never skip.
▶ Watch howFlora: light band — gold for everyone
6. Dumbbell Floor Press 2 × 10 · optional
Lie on the floor (knees bent), a dumbbell in each hand, press straight up. The floor stops your elbows — that protects the shoulder.
Works: chest, triceps. Why: chest strength without the bench-press shoulder risk.
▶ Watch howFlora: skip or very light

⚡ Finisher · optional, 5–8 min

Modified Burpee 3 × 8
Step (don't jump) back into a plank, step back in, stand. No push-up needed.
Why: the ultimate "can I get up off the floor" test.
▶ Watch howFlora: hands on a counter (incline)
Dumbbell Swing 3 × 12
Hinge at the hips (like the RDL) and swing the dumbbell to chest height using your HIPS, not your arms.
Why: power & conditioning, easy on the joints.
▶ Watch how
Farmer's Carry 3 × 30 sec
A dumbbell in each hand, stand tall, walk the room. Grip hard, brace the belly.
Why: the most real-life exercise there is — grip strength predicts a long life.
▶ Watch howFlora: lighter, shorter walk

🧘 Cool-down · 5 min

Broom pass-throughs · 2×10
Finish the shoulder where you started it.
Stretch
Hamstrings, hip-flexors, gentle chest/shoulder — hold each 20–30 sec, breathe in.
2 min slow breathing
Long exhales — the windpipe feeling. Bring the engine down.
