⚠️ Shoulder rules — read first

  1. Pain-free range only. Stretch/burn = good. Sharp pinch = stop, no ego.
  2. Neutral grip (palms facing) overhead. No behind-neck, no heavy flat bench.
  3. Warm it first (broom), feed it every session (face pulls).
  4. Smart-training, not medical advice. Real injury? Get it looked at.

🔥 Warm-up · 5–8 min

✓
Easy movement 2–3 min
March on the spot, step-ups, or the bike — just get the blood moving and a light sweat going.
✓
Broomstick shoulder pass-throughs 2 × 10
Hold a broom wide with both hands, slowly raise it up over your head and back behind you, then return. Like Leonardo's measured motion.
Why: opens & warms the shoulder safely before you load it.
▶ Watch howFlora: narrow your grip if tight, never force
✓
Band pull-aparts 2 × 15
Hold a band in front, arms straight, pull it apart across your chest, squeeze your shoulder blades together.
Why: the daily "shoulder vitamin" — wakes the rotator cuff, fixes the hunch.
▶ Watch howFlora: a towel works too, small range is fine
✓
Bodyweight squats + hip hinges 2×10 + 1×10
Squats: sit down & stand up, no weight. Hinges: soft knees, push hips back, hands slide down thighs.
Why: greases the knees & hips for the real lifts.

💪 The Workout · full body

Straight sets · slow down / controlled up · rest 60–90 sec · leave 2 reps in the tank.

✓
1. Goblet Squat 3 × 10
Hold one dumbbell at your chest with both hands. Sit down like into a chair, stand back up tall.
Works: legs, glutes, core. Why: the king of staying strong getting off chairs & floors — independence itself.
▶ Watch howFlora: no weight — just sit-to-stand from a real chair. That counts.
✓
2. Romanian Deadlift (RDL) 3 × 10
Dumbbells in hands, soft knees, push your hips BACK (not down), lower the weights along your legs till you feel the hamstrings stretch, then stand tall.
Works: hamstrings, glutes, low back. Why: trains the hinge that protects your back when you lift things. (You deadlifted yesterday — keep these LIGHT.)
▶ Watch howFlora: no/light weight, hands sliding down thighs, small range
✓
3. One-Arm Dumbbell Row 3 × 10 / side
One hand & knee on the bench. Other hand pulls the dumbbell up to your hip — like pull-starting a lawnmower.
Works: back, lats. Why: pulling strength & posture — balances all the front-of-body stuff.
▶ Watch howFlora: light, brace on a sturdy chair or table
✓
4. Neutral-Grip Overhead Press 2–3 × 8
Dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing each other. Press up only as high as feels good, lower slowly.
Works: shoulders, triceps. Why: overhead strength (top shelves) — neutral grip is the shoulder-safe way. Stop at any pinch.
▶ Watch howFlora: seated, light, tiny range
✓
5. Face Pulls 3 × 15
Anchor a band at face height. Pull both ends toward your face, elbows high & wide, squeeze the back of your shoulders.
Works: rear shoulders + rotator cuff. Why: 👈 THE shoulder-saver — undoes the phone/desk hunch. Never skip.
▶ Watch howFlora: light band, slow — this one's gold for everyone
✓
6. Dumbbell Floor Press 2 × 10 · optional
Lie on the floor (knees bent), a dumbbell in each hand, press straight up. The floor stops your elbows — that's what protects the shoulder.
Works: chest, triceps. Why: chest strength without the risk of a full bench press. Skip if the shoulder complains.
▶ Watch howFlora: skip, or very light

⚡ Finisher · optional, 5–8 min

✓
Modified Burpee 3 × 8
Step (don't jump) back into a plank, step back in, stand up. No push-up needed.
Why: the ultimate "can I get up off the floor" test — functional gold.
▶ Watch howFlora: hands on a counter/chair (incline) instead of the floor
✓
Dumbbell Swing 3 × 12
Hinge at the hips (like the RDL) and swing the dumbbell to chest height using your HIPS, not your arms.
Why: power & conditioning, easy on the joints.
▶ Watch howFlora: optional, light, small swing
✓
Farmer's Carry 3 × 30 sec
A dumbbell in each hand, stand tall, walk the room. Grip hard, brace the belly.
Why: the most real-life exercise there is (carrying the shopping) — grip strength predicts a long life.
▶ Watch howFlora: lighter, shorter walk — groceries count

🧘 Cool-down · 5 min

✓
Broom pass-throughs · 2×10
Same as the warm-up — finish the shoulder where you started it.
✓
Stretch
Hamstrings, hip-flexors, gentle chest/shoulder — hold each 20–30 sec, breathe into it.
✓
2 min slow breathing
Long exhales — the windpipe feeling. Bring the engine down before you climb the stairs.
