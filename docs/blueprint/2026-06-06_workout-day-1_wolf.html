⚠️ Shoulder Commandments — read first

  1. Pain-free range only. Stretch/burn = fine. Sharp pinch = stop, no ego.
  2. Neutral grip (palms facing) for anything overhead.
  3. No behind-the-neck, no heavy flat bench, no wide grip.
  4. Warm it first (broom), feed it every session (face pulls).
  5. Smart-training plan, not medical advice — real injury? get it looked at.

🔥 Warm-up · 5–8 min

  • ✓Easy movement 2–3 minmarch / step-ups / bike — until warm
  • ✓Broom shoulder pass-throughs · 2×10wide grip, slow, pain-free arc — the Leonardo move
  • ✓Band pull-aparts · 2×15wake the rear delts + rotator cuff
  • ✓Bodyweight squats 2×10 + hip hinges 1×10grease knees & hips
