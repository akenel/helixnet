FARMACIA -- Pharmacy

"Buongiorno. Ho avuto la diarrea stanotte. Cosa mi consiglia?"
Good morning. I had diarrhea last night. What do you recommend?
bwon-JOR-no. oh ah-VOO-toh la dee-ah-REH-ah sta-NOT-teh. KO-za mee kon-SEEL-ya?

What to ask for:

1. Imodium (loperamide)
Stops diarrhea. Same name in Italian.
"Vorrei Imodium, per favore"
vor-RAY ee-MO-dee-um
2. Electrolyte sachets
Replaces salt + minerals lost overnight
"Sali minerali reidratanti"
SA-lee mee-neh-RA-lee ray-ee-dra-TAN-tee
3. Biochetasi
Italian digestive tablets (effervescent). Very common.
"Biochetasi, per favore"
bee-oh-keh-TA-zee
If they ask questions:

"Da quando?" (Since when?) -- "Da stanotte" (Since last night)
"Ha mangiato qualcosa di strano?" (Did you eat something strange?) -- "Non lo so" (I don't know)
"Ha la febbre?" (Do you have a fever?) -- "No, niente febbre" (No, no fever)

Pharmacy location:

Ask at reception: "Dov'e' la farmacia piu' vicina?"
(Where is the nearest pharmacy?)
doh-VEH la far-MA-chee-ah pyoo vee-CHEE-na?

DOPO -- After Pharmacy

Take Imodium as directed on the box
Usually 2 tablets first, then 1 after each episode
Dissolve electrolyte sachet in water bottle
Sip throughout the day
Lunch: plain pasta with olive oil only
Pasta in bianco con olio d'oliva
Rest. Siesta. Cards tomorrow.
Riposare. Le cartoline possono aspettare.
If feeling good after pharmacy: walk past ISOTTO or Mixology -- but no pressure. Just seeds. Be water.
