Day 2 — Catania → Etna (Nicolosi) PATCHED

A18 / SS121 → SP92  ·  ~45 km  ·  ~1h  ·  Shortest drive day — slow morning

Why the flip

Sylvie tired + already east = drop the long day, do Etna tomorrow as a slow morning. Short drive, warmer nap, volcano dessert.

Plan

  • Slowest coffee of the trip — she earned it
  • Fuel up in Catania plain BEFORE climbing (no stations on SP92)
  • Roll when she's ready (11:30–12:00 fine)
  • Climb SP92 via Nicolosi — 2.2m no issue
  • Optional: cable car / jeep tour up to crater from Rifugio Sapienza
  • Lava fields walk — easy, otherworldly

Park-up

Primary: Nicolosi sosta area — ~37.6150, 15.0280 — 700m, warmer
Alternate: Rifugio Sapienza (1900m) — crater-proximate, COLD, layers mandatory

⚠ Etna rules: Top up fuel in Catania plain before SP92. Weather shifts fast at altitude — real jacket even if sunny at base.
