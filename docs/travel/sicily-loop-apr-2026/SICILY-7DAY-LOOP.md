## Day 2 — Catania → Etna (Nicolosi) — PATCHED

**Route:** A18/SS121 → SP92
**Distance:** ~45 km | **Drive time:** ~1h (short!) | **Elevation:** sea → 700m

### Why the flip
Sylvie tired + already east = **drop the long day, do Etna tomorrow as a slow morning**. Short drive, warmer nap, volcano dessert.

### Morning
- [ ] Slowest coffee of the trip — she earned it
- [ ] Fuel up in Catania plain BEFORE climbing (no stations on SP92)
- [ ] Roll when she's ready (11:30–12:00 fine)

### Daytime
- [ ] Climb SP92 via Nicolosi — 2.2m no issue, road widens
- [ ] Arrive Nicolosi by early afternoon
- [ ] Optional: cable car / jeep tour up to crater from Rifugio Sapienza
- [ ] Lava fields walk — easy, otherworldly

### Park-up
- **Primary:** Nicolosi sosta area — **~37.6150, 15.0280** — 700m, warmer
- **Alternate:** Rifugio Sapienza lot (1900m) — crater-proximate, **COLD**, layers mandatory
- **Fuel rule:** top up in Catania plain before SP92. No stations uphill.

### ⚠️ Etna Notes
- Weather shifts fast at altitude — pack a real jacket even if sunny at base
- Sunrise from Sapienza = legendary (if you commit to the cold)
- Park4night has a few free wild spots on SP92 shoulder — verify

## 2.2m Width Reminders

- Historic centers = ZTL = walk in. Never drive in.
- Park OUTSIDE: Taormina, Ortigia, Ragusa Ibla, Cefalù centro
- SP roads interior Ragusa province = tight, avoid after dark
- A19 / A18 / A29 autostrade = no width issue
- Fuel stations: use "truck" pumps where available, they're wider
