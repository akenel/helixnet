The four rules — locked Angel: "your leans are fine" · 2026-07-11

① Artemis match
Exact name → auto-accept. Fuzzy → show candidate, operator confirms (anti-misassignment).
② "Your price" default
Pre-fill from the Artemis price — it's literally what Felix sells today. Adjust freely.
③ Field ownership on conflict
EAN ← 420 · category / 18+ / VAT / tiers ← Artemis · name/description ← whichever's cleaner.
④ Status states
matched-both · 420-only (no Artemis) · manual · not-found.
🔑 Why this is the migration accelerator: the operator never builds a product — they scan → confirm → price. Identity, category, 18+, VAT and tiers all arrive pre-filled from the two databases we already own. Anyone can do it accurately, fast, and every price stays on the record for Felix to compare later.
