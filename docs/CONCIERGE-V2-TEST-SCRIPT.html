How to use: Log in first (link below), then work top to bottom. Tick each check as you confirm it, jot anything odd in the comment box under each section, and hit Copy report at the end to paste your feedback back to Tigs. Your ticks & notes auto-save in this browser. no prod — local only

Best on Chrome (the microphone uses Chrome's speech engine). On your phone, open the same URL in Chrome to test the mic with the phone keyboard mic.

0 · Log in

Everything below needs you logged into the Bottega.

Open the Concierge ↗ https://helix.local/compute/concierge
  1. Click Log in, sign in, land back on Cleopatra's page. you see the chat, not the "log in" card

1 · The board is masters-only grounding

The dispatcher can only route to real masters. Your family was seeded as "legends" — they must NOT be on the public board.

Open the Legends wall ↗ https://helix.local/compute/legends
  1. Search the wall for Kenel. Dave / Mario / Paul Kenel do NOT appear as masters
  2. Browse a House or two (e.g. The Lyceum, The Atelier). only real historical masters — Einstein, Curie, da Vinci, etc.
  3. No obvious newbies / regular members on the wall. it reads like a hall of legends, not a member list

2 · Cleopatra greets & settles the language

She's the front door: five-star welcome, language-first, keeps "in the door, or out the door?".

Open the Concierge ↗
  1. Fresh visit shows an opening greeting. "we have been expecting you…", offers EN/IT, keeps the door line
  2. Type in English, send. she replies in English and asks about your language comfort
  3. Set "Reply in" to IT and send a line. she replies in Italian
  4. Tap a suggestion chip under the box. it sends that line for you

3 · The microphone 🎤 talk to the machine

Speak instead of type — transcribed in the browser, nothing uploaded. The big one for mobile.

  1. Click 🎤 (bottom-left of the box), allow mic if asked. it turns red + pulses, shows "listening…"
  2. Say a sentence. your words appear in the box as you speak
  3. Click 🎤 again to stop, edit the text, then Send. stops cleanly; you can fix the text before sending
  4. Set "Reply in" to IT, mic on, speak Italian. it transcribes Italian (lang follows the picker)
  5. On your phone (Chrome): same page, tap 🎤, talk. phone mic works, hands-free input

4 · The roomy composer

No more cramped one-liner — a real editor.

  1. Box is ~3 lines tall and grows as you type a long message. grows up to a limit, then scrolls — never tiny
  2. Press Shift+Enter. adds a new line (does NOT send)
  3. Press Enter. sends the message
  4. Type something, watch the char count, click 🗑️. count shows; trash clears the box

5 · The Dispatcher 🏛️ the handoff

Cleo packages your question and hands it to the 2 best masters on the board — they answer in their own voice.

Open the Concierge ↗
  1. Type a real question (e.g. "How do I find courage to start over at 60?") and click 🏛️ Masters. a "dispatched to the court" block with TWO master cards
  2. Each card shows a name, a House, a "why this one", and an answer. real masters (not made-up names), answers in their voice
  3. Click 🔊 on a master card. it reads that master's answer aloud
  4. Set "Reply in" IT, dispatch an Italian question. the masters answer in Italian
  5. Try a nonsense / off-topic question. it still picks real masters or gracefully says it can't — never invents a wizard
  6. Variety: ask 3 different-topic questions (business, music, health). DIFFERENT, fitting masters each time — e.g. Carnegie/Sun Tzu, Beethoven/Mozart, Hippocrates/Nightingale — not the same two every time

6 · The Scorecard 📊

Three honest numbers so a member sees where they're at.

Open Your dashboard ↗ https://helix.local/compute/me
  1. On the Home tab, a "your scorecard" card shows 3 numbers: Portrait %, Things made, Masters consulted. three stats + a progress bar
  2. After chatting with Cleopatra, the Portrait % rises. more you tell her → higher %
  3. After dispatching questions, Masters consulted reflects it. count matches the masters you reached
  4. Click "Tell Cleopatra more →". jumps you back to the Concierge

7 · Saved handoff renders right the bug you found

The dispatch you save must display as readable master cards in the workshop — never "[object Object]".

Open the Workshop ↗
  1. Dispatch a question, then in the output click 📓 Save to Blueprint. saves without error
  2. Open it again from your Blueprint / history. the masters show as cards: name · house · why · answer — NOT "[object Object]"
  3. Check 📖 Reading mode + 📋 Copy on the saved handoff. both produce readable text, masters included

8 · Robustness spot-checks no silly errors

The little things that throw 400s if we're sloppy.

  1. Empty box: Send + 🏛️ Masters are disabled. can't send nothing
  2. Send a very long multi-paragraph message. handled, no error, scrolls
  3. Switch Auto / EN / IT a few times mid-chat. replies follow the picker, no flip-flop
  4. Reload the page mid-conversation. your chat + record are still there
