Felix on the Road

~95s · Syd Field three acts · the shop in your pocket — the freedom to not be there

The story (read first)

Felix owns Artemis but is almost never in it. The 3am question every owner knows — what's happening in my shop right now? — answered in thirty seconds on his phone: today's sales, Pam on the till, the drawer balanced, the cream selling down. The PRD badge says it's the real shop, live. He catches what's off from the highway, or closes the phone and drives on. The freedom nobody sells you with a till: the freedom to not be there.

How to read it: calm, a little wistful, then steady — this is peace of mind, and the button is the heart (the freedom to leave). One continuous take, ~1s on the ⏸ HOLD beats. Hand it back, I drive the screens.
