# Born Once #06 — "Felix on the Road" (script)

*The cream's **watched** chapter — and the show's most personal one. Felix owns Artemis but is
almost never in it. The drama every owner knows: what's happening in my shop when I'm not there?
Banco's answer: the whole shop, in his pocket. Theme: the freedom to leave and still have it.
New pipeline: Angel reads, Tigs drives every screen. Target ~95s. Tone: calm, a little wistful,
then steady — peace of mind.*

**Teaching point:** remote ownership. The owner sees everything from his phone — today's sales,
who's on the till, the drawer, the cream's velocity — live, real (the **PRD** badge), no calling
in and interrupting the floor. The watching is built in, so the owner can actually leave.

**Continuity:** the box arrived in #05; here Felix watches it sell down from the road. Small loop closed.

---

## Syd Field, three acts (~95s)
- **ACT 1** (0–20s): the 3am question — what's happening in my shop right now?
- **ACT 2** (20–62s): the turn — thirty seconds on his phone, the whole shop breathing; the badge says it's real.
- **ACT 3** (62–95s): he catches what's off or closes the phone → the button (the freedom to not be there).

---

## The script — cards + VO

**ACT 1 · COLD OPEN**
**CARD:** *Every owner's 3am question.*
**VO:** "Here's the question that wakes up every shop owner at three in the morning: what's happening in my shop right now — when I'm not standing in it?"
*(Puppeteer: a phone-shaped dashboard, dim)*

**ACT 1 · FELIX ISN'T THERE**
**CARD:** *Felix is almost never in the shop.*
**VO:** "Felix owns Artemis. But he's almost never behind the counter — he's on the road, between a hundred other things. So how does he sleep at night? He opens his phone."

**ACT 2 · THE TURN — the shop in his pocket**
**CARD:** *The whole shop, in his pocket.*
**VO:** "Thirty seconds. Today's sales. Who's on the till — that's Pam. The drawer, counted and balanced. And the cream from yesterday's delivery? Selling down, tube by tube, exactly like it should. He's two hundred kilometres away, and he can watch his shop breathing."
*(Puppeteer: Felix's dashboard — sales, the drawer, velocity)*

**ACT 2 · THE BADGE — it's real**
**CARD:** *PRD. Live. The real shop.*
**VO:** "And that little badge in the corner — PRD — tells him this is the real shop, live. Not a test, not yesterday. The numbers are now. He's not guessing, and he's not calling in to interrupt Pam mid-sale. He just… knows."
*(Puppeteer: zoom the status-bar env badge)*

**ACT 3 · RESOLUTION**
**CARD:** *He sees it before he's asked.*
**VO:** "If something's off, he catches it from the highway — and sends one text. If everything's fine, he closes the phone and keeps driving. Either way, the shop didn't need him standing inside it to be safe."

**ACT 3 · THE BUTTON**
**CARD:** *An owner who can leave.*
**VO:** "That's the freedom nobody sells you with a cash register — the freedom to not be there. A shop that runs while you're gone, because the watching is built in. Felix can finally leave the shop… and still have it."

**OUTRO**
**CARD:** *BORN ONCE — the shop in your pocket.*
