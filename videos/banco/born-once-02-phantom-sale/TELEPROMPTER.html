The Phantom Sale

A true-crime short · ~90s · Syd Field three acts · the footage is REAL (your Fairphone, this morning)

The story (read first)

A cashier makes a brand-new product, sells it — and two minutes later it's gone. The sale was real, the money was real, but the product never saved. A phantom. We hunt it down to one silent permission error — one seal worked, its identical twin leaked — and kill it in one line, before it ever reached a real shop. Hook → turn → hunt → catch → lesson.

How to read it: read the VO lines aloud. This is a thriller — when you see a ⏸ HOLD tag, STOP and let the silence sit (1–2 seconds). The pauses ARE the suspense. Don't rush the reveals.
