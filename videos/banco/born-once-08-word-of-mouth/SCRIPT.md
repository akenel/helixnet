# Born Once #08 — "Word of Mouth" (script · SEASON 1 FINALE + SEASON 2 TEASER)

*The honest finale. Season 1 measured everything about the cream EXCEPT the one thing that
matters — did it work? Only the people who took it home know. So #08 wraps Season 1 and **teases
Season 2**: the customer feedback loop — QR on the receipt → a 10-second survey → points → the
cream's reputation → Felix sees what *helped*, not just what sold. We're honest that we're BUILDING
this next, not claiming it's live. Tone: warm, building to a "here's what's coming" lift. ~115s.*

**Why honest:** the loyalty/credits engine is ~85% built (CustomerModel, credits ledger, sale
attaches a customer). The missing last mile — product reviews + media + the on-product display — is
Season 2's build. So we say *"here's how it's going to work,"* not *"here's how it works."*

---

## The script — cards + VO

**ACT 1 · SEASON 1 IN A BREATH**
**CARD:** *One cream. Eight chapters.*
**VO:** "One little cream. Born on a Friday, rough and nameless. Sold. Made proper. Restocked. Watched from the road. Counted to the franc. Banco measured all of it. But there's one thing the numbers still can't tell us — did it actually *work*? Was it worth forty francs?"

**ACT 1 · ONLY THEY KNOW**
**CARD:** *Only Larry and Sally know.*
**VO:** "Only the people who took it home can answer that — Larry, Sally, the next nervous customer holding the tube. So in Season Two, we hand them the pen."

**ACT 2 · IT STARTS ON THE RECEIPT**
**CARD:** *It starts on the receipt.*
**VO:** "Here's how it's going to work. They never give us an email — they never do. A first name, maybe an Instagram. That's enough. Because on their receipt is a little QR code. They scan it — no app, no password — and up comes one quick question: did it work?"

**ACT 2 · TEN SECONDS**
**CARD:** *Ten seconds. A few honest taps.*
**VO:** "Ten seconds. A thumbs up. A star. 'Would you buy it again?' Maybe a photo — or a ten-second clip of it calming the bite. And for their honesty, they earn points. Real credit, toward their next visit."

**ACT 2 · THE CREAM GETS A VOICE**
**CARD:** *Now the cream has a voice.*
**VO:** "Those words don't vanish into a database. They land on the cream itself. So the next person who hesitates doesn't just see a price — they see Larry. They see Sally. They see 'it worked.' The cream stops selling on Felix's word, and starts selling on the neighbourhood's."

**ACT 3 · FELIX SEES WHAT HELPED**
**CARD:** *What helped — not just what sold.*
**VO:** "And Felix, from the road, finally sees the whole picture — not just what *sold*, but what *helped*. The velocity, and the reason behind it. That's the loop: born once, then known — by the people who carried it home."

**ACT 3 · THE BUTTON — SEASON 2**
**CARD:** *Season 2 — the customers pick up the pen.*
**VO:** "We're building it right now. Season Two of Banco — where the shop stops talking, and the neighbourhood starts. Born once. Known forever."

**OUTRO**
**CARD:** *BORN ONCE — Season 2, coming soon.*
