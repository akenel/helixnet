## What you need to do (in order)

**1. Create the playlist (one time):**
- YouTube Studio → **Playlists** → **New playlist**
- Name: **Born Once**
- Description: *"Short films on Banco — a Swiss-made till built for the counter, not the warehouse. Every product is born once, then known forever."*
- Visibility: **Public**

**2. Upload the Short:**
- Upload `Banco-BornOnce-01-Zero-Perpetual-Inventory.mp4` (portrait + under 3 min = a Short automatically)
- **Title:** paste your chosen title from above
- **Description:** open `DESCRIPTION.txt`, select all, paste
- **Thumbnail:** upload `thumbnail.png` (it shows on your channel + playlist; the Shorts feed itself autoplays, so it won't always appear there — upload it anyway)
- **Tags:** open `TAGS.txt`, copy, paste into the Tags field
- **Playlist:** tick **Born Once**
- **Audience:** "No, not made for kids"
- **Visibility:** Public → **Publish**

**3. Sanity check after publish:**
- Plays vertical/full-screen on a phone (it's a Short)
- Audio: your voice clear, music a faint bed underneath
- Title hook reads in the first glance
