One line of code.

That's all it took to fix a production bug that broke every single button in our client's app.

No background colors. No hover effects. No rounded corners. Just sad, naked HTML sitting there doing nothing.

The culprit?

<style>

Should have been:

<style type="text/tailwindcss">

The Tailwind CDN needs that type attribute to process @apply directives. Without it, every .btn-primary, .btn-secondary, and .btn-success class gets silently ignored. No errors. No warnings. Just... nothing.

I recorded the entire fix live. No scripts, no staging, no second takes.
