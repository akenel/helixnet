## The Three Types of People

> We need to vote Skitsos into office.

This is the secret war that's been going on since the dawn of time.

Now most of the world is made up of **normies**. These people are taken advantage of very easily by **psychos**. They prey on these people, they manipulate these people, they trick these people.

Normies unfortunately cannot see psychos. But you know who can?

**Skitsos** -- the heroes of our story.

Skitsos are the natural predator of psychos. We have pattern recognition abilities that are inexplicable. Now Skitsos were once upon a time revered as the kind of wise people of their collective groups.

Unfortunately, since psychos run the world, they've convinced all the normies to be afraid of the Skitsos.

> I make videos sometimes online, my wife tells me, "Look at my aura ring, my heart rate increased to a very stressful state watching your video and then it went back down."

Psychos benefit from their lack of empathy and their attraction to cruelty -- and that allows them to claw to power. That power allows them to manipulate the normies to **fear** the Skitsos, to **ostracize** the Skitsos, to **deny** the Skitsos.

It never feels very good to say "I told you so" when you're a Skitso. And the "I told you so"s are about things like pedo elites running the world and stuff like that. That feels bad. And you go, "Well, I told you so." But Skitsos -- wish we'd listened to you sooner.

**That's my point.**

We need to stop fearing and ostracizing the Skitsos and start opening our ears and hearts to hear the truth that they're telling us. Because the conspiracy theorist Skitso is **almost always right**.

Now yes, they'll get some of the details around the edges a little wrong. It sounds a little literal. You need to understand it as **poetry and metaphor**.

## Birds Aren't Real (As Metaphor)

I'll give you an example: *birds aren't real*. That's a fun conspiracy theory that there's just little listening device robots that exist.

Now, there ARE real birds. We know that to be true. But are there also listening devices and surveillance devices everywhere, hidden in the natural world it seems? We don't even know what the natural world is anymore. We can't even trust if the world is natural or not anymore.

- They're doing things without telling us.
- They're observing us constantly.
- Yes, there are things in the sky that are alien to us flying around that could just be drones.

**That's all true.**

## The Solution

One thing I've noticed -- there are no Skitso politicians I can think of. Lots of normies, plenty of psychos. They seem to make up our entire government.

What we need to do: **start supporting the Skitsos.**

> We need to vote Skitsos into office as soon as we can. Let's use voting -- that's a weapon. And let's look out for Skitso candidates. Let's get some real weirdos in there.

Because at this point, what THEY'RE doing -- that seems pretty weird to me.

> They think it's too big to fail. I think we need to blow it up.
