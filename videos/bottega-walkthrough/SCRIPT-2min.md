# La Piazza · Bottega — 2-Minute Walkthrough Script

*Filmed side-by-side: this script (or `teleprompter.html`) on the LEFT screen, the live app on the RIGHT.*
*Target runtime: 2:00. Voiceover ≈ 230 words — speak slower than feels natural, pause on every click.*

---

## ☐ PRE-FLIGHT — do all of this BEFORE you press record

1. **Right screen:** logged in at `bottega.lapiazza.app`. **Left screen:** this teleprompter.
2. **CREDITS CHECK** — top-right of the app must show a **positive** number. The last take died on
   `not enough credits − 18`. If it's negative, top up first. Do NOT record a recipe you can't run.
3. **App window NARROW** (phone width) on the right — it looks best *and* crops cleanly to Shorts later.
4. **Ask Cleo → "Start fresh"** so the chat is clean and the card starts where you want it.
5. **Write your ONE opening sentence** on a sticky (example in Scene 2). Decide it now, don't improvise on camera.
6. **Pick your recipe** for Scene 4 and confirm it runs (positive credits). Default: **Find Your Edge**.
7. **Mic check** — say one line, play it back, confirm it's not the desktop/ambient.
8. **Close other tabs / notifications.** One clean browser, one clean run.

> Golden rule from the last take: **if anything errors on camera, stop, reload, start the scene again.**
> Never narrate over a red error. We cut clean takes, we don't ship the bumps.

---

## SCENE 1 — The promise · 0:00–0:15

**DO:** Start on the **La Piazza Square** (home). Scroll down once, slowly, then back to top.

**SAY:**
> "This is La Piazza. If a system ever judged you without reading your story —
> this is the opposite of that. No forms. No CV upload. You just talk."

---

## SCENE 2 — Meet Cleo · 0:15–0:35

**DO:** Click **Ask Cleo** in the top nav. Click into the chat box.

**SAY:**
> "This is Cleopatra — the front desk. I tell her one true thing about me."

**DO:** Type your sentence. Press **Send**.

> *Example sentence (use your own real one):*
> *"I've spent fifteen years deep in SAP integration, and I want to start working for myself."*

---

## SCENE 3 — She listens, the card fills · 0:35–1:05

**DO:** Let Cleo reply. Tap **one** answer chip, or type a short reply.

**SAY:**
> "She doesn't interrogate me. She listens, asks one sharp question back,
> and writes my card while we talk."

**DO:** Click **You** (your profile / My Rebuild) to open the portrait.

**SAY:**
> "Two minutes of conversation — and here's a real profile.
> Most of it written from a short chat, in my own words.
> That's a bio I'd actually send to a client."

---

## SCENE 4 — She points to the move · 1:05–1:40

**SAY:**
> "And she doesn't leave me standing there.
> She points me to the move that fits what I already have."

**DO:** Click **Workshop**. Pick **Find Your Edge** (or Cover Letter / CV → Bio — any recipe you have credits for).

**SAY:**
> "Everything here is a recipe. Pick one. Answer a couple of questions. Get the thing."

**DO:** Fill the one field. Click **▶ Run recipe**.

**SAY:**
> "A few seconds on a shared brain…"

**DO:** Wait for the **OUTPUT** to appear. Scroll it gently so the result is readable.

**SAY:**
> "…and that's mine. Written for me, ready to use."

---

## SCENE 5 — The close · 1:40–2:00

**DO:** Scroll once to show the footer line —
*"every recipe is a menu entry, not an app · we host the square, not the model"* — then back to the output.

**SAY:**
> "No algorithm deciding who I am. No monthly ransom. My data stays mine.
> That's the whole idea — a town square where everybody gets a five-star welcome,
> not just the ones who already fit in.
> Come find your leverage. This is La Piazza."

**END · ~2:00**
