# PAM ATTACK #001 — The Coffee Machine Betrayal

## Setup
Monday morning. Pam arrives early. Felix left a note:
"New coffee machine installed. Good luck. - F"

## The Attack
1. Customer walks in
2. Pam: "Would you like a coffee?"
3. Customer: "Sure, cappuccino please"
4. Pam presses button
5. Machine makes GRINDING DEATH SOUND
6. Nothing comes out
7. Pam presses again
8. Machine displays: "ENTKALKEN" (descale in German)
9. Pam doesn't speak German
10. Customer: "Is everything okay?"
11. Pam: *sweating* "Just... one moment..."
12. Pam Telegrams Felix: "MACHINE SAYS ENTKALKEN WTF"
13. Felix (driving): "Google it"
14. Customer checks watch
15. Pam Googles. Sees "descale."
16. Pam: "It needs... descaling?"
17. Customer: "I'll just take a tea."
18. Pam: *relieved* "EXCELLENT CHOICE!"

## UAT Points Tested
- Multi-language error handling (DE→EN)
- Customer recovery flow
- Telegram emergency communication
- Graceful product substitution
