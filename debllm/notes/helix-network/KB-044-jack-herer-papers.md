## Who is Jack Herer?

Jack Herer (1939-2010) was an American cannabis activist and author.
His book *"The Emperor Wears No Clothes"* (1985) is the bible of hemp advocacy.

The Jack Herer cannabis strain is named after him.
So are the rolling papers Joey is looking for.
