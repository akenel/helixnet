## The Edge Cases Felix Handles

### Early Arrival + Coffee
> "Sometimes they will come 20 minutes, maybe an hour, and chill, drink coffee, and then start."

**Felix's approach**: Trust them to log start time when they actually START working, not when they walk in.

### Overtime for Cleaning
> "Some days they work overtime to clean and re-sort shelves for 20 or 30 minutes."

**Felix's approach**: They log it, he pays it. Simple.

### The Cash Theft Risk
> "I can see them stealing cash but I have the camera - not only for customers stealing stuff but that exact problem."

**Felix's approach**: Camera is the deterrent. Trust but verify.

## Why POS Login Isn't Enough

Angel suggested: "Clock in = login, clock out = closeout"

Felix's response: "Not so simple."

| Scenario | POS Login/Logout | Reality |
|----------|------------------|---------|
| Arrive early, coffee first | Would miss 20-60 min | Paid for it |
| Stay late to clean shelves | Would miss 20-30 min | Paid for it |
| Quick break outside | Still logged in | Still working |

**The gap**: POS tracks SALES time, not WORK time. Cleaning shelves isn't a transaction.

## The Family Trust Factor

> "The Artemis staff would have a pretty hard time faking extra hours. It naturally would not happen."

Why:
- Small team, everyone knows each other
- Felix is there daily
- Camera as psychological deterrent
- Probation period filters early
- Leandra example: "Great attitude, has interests, loves working at Artemis"

---

## Leandra's Probation

> "She is still on probation time and she also has to decide if the job is right for her."

Swiss probation period = mutual evaluation:
- Employer evaluates employee
- Employee evaluates job fit
- Both can exit easily during this time

This natural filter prevents Hanksters from ever getting permanent.

---

## The Hybrid Solution (Future)

For Felix's scale:
1. **Optional clock-in/out buttons** (not mandatory)
2. **POS activity as baseline** (proof of presence)
3. **Manual override for non-sales time** (cleaning, setup)
4. **5-minute block rounding** (honor Felix's system)
5. **Trust the employee input** (like today)

The system augments Felix's approach, doesn't replace it.
