## ðŸ”§ Warranty & Returns

### Warranty Terms (Storz & Bickel)
- **Duration:** 3 years (manufacturer)
- **Coverage:** Manufacturing defects, heating element, electronics
- **Not Covered:** Wear parts (bags, screens, O-rings), user damage
- **Process:** Customer brings to store â†’ we ship to Storz & Bickel â†’ replaced within 2-4 weeks

### Return Policy (Artemis)
- **Unopened:** 14 days, full refund
- **Opened:** No return (hygiene product - Swiss law)
- **Defective on arrival:** Immediate exchange

### Handling Warranty Claims
1. Check receipt (must have purchase date, our store)
2. Inspect device (obvious user damage?)
3. If covered: Document issue, give customer tracking
4. If not covered: Explain wear parts, offer to sell replacements
