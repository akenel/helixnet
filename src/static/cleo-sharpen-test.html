What you're testing: after Cleopatra has learned a little about you, a 🎯 "Narrow it down" card offers a few either/or questions — ranked so each answer sharpens your interest profile the most. The promise: each answer visibly tightens the Interests (RIASEC) bars in the right-hand panel.

0 · Log in to staging

Everything below needs you logged into the Bottega on staging.

Open the Concierge (staging) ↗ https://staging-bottega.lapiazza.app/compute/concierge
  1. Log in (angel / helix_pass), land back on Cleopatra's page. you see the chat, not the "log in" card

1 · Build a base portrait first prerequisite

Sharpen only makes sense once she knows something — that's by design. Give her a couple of real things about you.

  1. Tell Cleopatra 2–3 real things (what you do, what you love, a goal). the right panel "What Cleopatra has learned" fills in (Goal, Background, Interests…)
  2. Watch the Interests (RIASEC) bars appear in the right panel. at least one or two interest bars show a value

2 · The 🎯 trigger appears the gate

Once she knows ≥2 things, a red-outlined invite appears above the message box. Not before — a blank chat has nothing to sharpen.

  1. Above the composer, a card reads "🎯 Narrow it down — a few quick questions to sharpen your profile". the trigger shows once she knows a couple of things about you
  2. Tap it. a brief "Lining up a couple of questions…", then a question card opens

3 · The questions feel like life, not a quiz grounding

Each is a real either/or about YOUR life. She must never name a test, a theme word, or anything invented.

  1. The card shows one question + TWO option buttons + a "1 of 3" counter + Done + "Skip this one". clean either/or, two concrete choices
  2. The question is about real life (e.g. "Dig into a hard puzzle, or sketch something new?"). a genuine life choice — not abstract
  3. It NEVER says "RIASEC", "Holland", "personality test", or scores. just life — no jargon, no test-speak
  4. It NEVER invents a master, House, room, lab, or program. nothing made-up sneaks into a question
  5. The questions feel relevant to what you told her. profile-aware, not random

4 · Each answer tightens the picture the whole point

This is the promise behind "narrow it down, more accurate": tapping an option re-scores your interests live.

  1. Before answering, glance at the Interests (RIASEC) bars in the right panel. note roughly where they sit
  2. Tap one option. Cleo replies in the chat, and the RIASEC bars shift — the side you leaned toward moves
  3. Answer the next ranked question (it advances to "2 of 3"). the bars keep refining with each answer
  4. After the batch, your top interests read sharper than at the start. the picture is more defined — "narrowed down"

5 · The flow controls behave

A small ranked batch — it should advance, skip, and close cleanly.

  1. The "N of 3" counter advances after each answer. 1 of 3 → 2 of 3 → 3 of 3
  2. Click "Skip this one". moves to the next question WITHOUT sending a chat message
  3. Click "Done" mid-batch. closes the card; the normal suggestion chips come back
  4. Finish the whole batch. card closes; the 🎯 trigger reappears so you can run a fresh round if you want

6 · It follows your language

An Italian conversation should get Italian questions — never English under Italian.

  1. Set "Reply in" to IT, then open 🎯 Sharpen. the either/or questions are in Italian
  2. Tap an Italian option. Cleo replies in Italian and the bars still update

7 · Edges & robustness no silly errors

The little things that break if we're sloppy.

  1. On a brand-new / empty chat, the 🎯 trigger is absent. nothing to sharpen until she knows you
  2. Instead of tapping, TYPE your own answer to a Sharpen question and Send. works fine — it's just a normal message; bars still update
  3. Reload the page mid-batch. your chat + record persist; the 🎯 trigger is still offered
  4. On your phone (Chrome): open Sharpen. card readable, the two options stack and are easy to tap
