## VISUAL GAG

The word "PIVOT" means to turn in place.
He's literally turning in place.
No forward movement.
That's the joke.

- **Papers flying** - chaos from the motion
- **Kid unmoved** - standing still, watching, timing
- **Tally board** - visual evidence of insanity
- **Dizziness spirals** - classic cartoon dizzy effect
