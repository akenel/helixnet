## CONCEPT

Everyone has a Score. It follows you everywhere.
Your Score decides if you get the loan, the apartment, the surgery.
Your Score is based on everything: sleep, purchases, steps, posts, friends.
Your Score is your worth. Your Score is your life.

But what happens when the Score is wrong?

---

## CHARACTER: MIKE

- 34 years old, works in accounting
- Always did everything "right"
- Score was 847 (excellent) his whole life
- One day, it drops to 412. No explanation.
- His life unravels in 48 hours.
