### THE SETUP

5:12 AM. Dave is outside. Walking. No GPS. No optimized route. Just walking toward an address on a piece of paper. The streets are empty. The city is still asleep. For the first time, he notices things: a cat on a windowsill, graffiti that says "WAKE UP", the smell of bread from a bakery. The world existed this whole time.
