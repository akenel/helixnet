## STYLE GUIDE

### Font: EDO SZ
- All HELIX logos
- Title cards
- "I DO NOT COMPLY" moments
- Dramatic captions

### Colors (when we add them):
- CTO: Grey suit, sponsor logos in corporate colors
- CHARLIE: Earth tones, third eye = Helix blue
- PAM: Bright, musical, rainbow vibes
- LEO: Orange/black stripes, blue brook water
- KID: Primary colors, simple
- SAL: Green (leafy), calm neutrals

### Panel Borders:
- Standard scenes: Simple black border
- CTO confusion: Wavy/wobbly border
- Eureka moments: No border (breakthrough!)
- Banksy moments: Spray paint edge effect
