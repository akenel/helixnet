Paper Stock Guide (ask ISOTTO what they have)

80-100gsm = Standard office paper (letters, drafts)
120-160gsm = Thick paper (brochures, displays)
200-250gsm = Card stock (postcards, business cards)
250-350gsm = Heavy card (premium postcards, tent cards) ← IDEAL for Job 1 & 2

Key Italian Words for the Print Shop

Carta fotografica = Photo paper
Carta lucida = Glossy paper
Carta opaca = Matte paper
Cartoncino = Card stock (thick)
Grammatura = Paper weight (gsm)
Stampa a colori = Color print
Scala 100% = 100% scale (no fit-to-page)
Un solo lato = Single-sided
Nessun margine = No margins / borderless
Quante copie? = How many copies?
