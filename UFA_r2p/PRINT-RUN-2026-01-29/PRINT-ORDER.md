## DUPLEX PRINT INSTRUCTIONS FOR FAMOSO

1. Open the PDF (e.g., `duplex-001-trapani.pdf`)
2. Print PAGE 1 (front side with images) on photo-quality paper
3. Take the printed sheet from the output tray
4. Feed it back into the printer face-down
5. Print PAGE 2 (back side with text/QR) on the blank side
6. Cut along the dashed line in the middle = 2 finished postcards
