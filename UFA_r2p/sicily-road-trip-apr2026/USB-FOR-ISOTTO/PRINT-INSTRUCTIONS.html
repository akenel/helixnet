⚠ MOST IMPORTANT SETTING · IMPOSTAZIONE PIÙ IMPORTANTE ⚠

DUPLEX: SHORT EDGE FLIP — VOLTARE SUL LATO CORTO

If duplex is set to "long edge" (lato lungo), the backs will print upside down behind the fronts. Always short edge flip for A4 landscape duplex.

Quality Checklist for Famous

Please verify before delivering:

  • Paper: matte cream cardstock 300gsm (NOT glossy, NOT thin)
  • Sheets: 2 sheets total, duplex (front + back on each)
  • Duplex flip: SHORT EDGE — fronts and backs aligned, backs right-side up
  • Cuts: clean lines, no jagged edges
  • Card size: each card 142.5 × 99mm (±1mm)
  • White border: 3mm visible on outer edges of each card after cut
  • Color: rich, no banding, no faded zones
  • Photos: sharp, not blurred
  • Text: crisp Italian + English quotes legible
